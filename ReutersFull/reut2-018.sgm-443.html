mitsubishi heavy industries ltd said it began building the world's most advanced energy-saving tanker, which consumes only 48 tonnes of fuel oil a day. construction of the 258,000 dwt vlcc (very large crude carrier) nisseki maru for , a shipping arm of nippon oil co ltd , is expected to be completed in april 1988. it would run on the japan/gulf route, a company statement said. the statement gave no other details. mitsubishi heavy last year finished building a vlcc of similar size, but which consumed 57 tonnes of fuel a day. reuter 
