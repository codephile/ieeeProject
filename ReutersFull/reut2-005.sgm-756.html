harris associates l.p., a chicago investment advisor, said it raised its stake in centerre bancorporation to 508,062 shares, or 6.6 pct of the total outstanding, from 427,061 shares, or 5.5 pct. in a filing with the securities and exchange commission, harris said it bought 81,001 centerre common shares between jan 2 and march 4 at prices ranging from 36.00 to 39.75 dlrs a share. it said its dealings in the company's stock are on behalf of its clients. reuter 
