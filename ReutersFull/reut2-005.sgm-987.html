waxman industries inc is raising 25 mln dlrs through an offering of convertible subordinated debentures due 2007 with a 6-1/4 pct coupon and par pricing, said sole underwriter drexel burnham lambert inc. the debentures are convertible into the company's common stock at 14.375 dlrs per share, representing a premium of 25 pct over the stock price when terms on the debt were set. moody's investors service inc rates the debentures b-2. proceeds will be used to repay short and long-term indebtedness. reuter 
