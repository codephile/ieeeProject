a group led by new york investor asher edelman said he lowered its stake in lucky stores inc to 1,827,734 shares, or 4.8 pct of the total outstanding common stock, from 1,930,734 shares, or 5.1 pct. in a filing with the securities and exchange commission, the edelman group said it sold 103,000 lucky stores common shares on march 9 and 10 at prices ranging from 28.625 to 30.375 dlrs a share. so long as edelman's stake is below five pct he is not required to report his dealings in lucky stores stock. reuter 
