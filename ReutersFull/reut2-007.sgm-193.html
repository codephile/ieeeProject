alcatel cit , the newly established french subsidiary of alcatel nv which now runs the worldwide telecommunications activities of itt corp , has won a 15 mln franc contract from the swedish armed forces for transmission equipment, alcatel said in a communique here. the order is for multiplexing equipment and falls within the framework of a 50 mln franc sales contract obtained by alcatel in 1982, it added. alcatel nv is a joint venture between itt and france's state-owned cie generale d'electricite . reuter 
