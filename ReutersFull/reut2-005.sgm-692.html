saft, a unit of cie generale d'electricite of france, said it bought u.k.-based alcad ltd from penn central corp's marathon manufacturing cos inc. terms of the deal were not disclosed. alcad is one of the world's largest producers of pocket-plate nickel-cadmium storeage batteries, used in industrial and railroad applications to start engines and as light sources, saft said. saft said it expects to add 400 jobs at the u.k. operations. reuter 
