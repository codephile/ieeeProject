bbc brown boveri und cie said it won a major order to supply its "comprex" pressure-wave supercharger for use in a new car diesel engine to be produced by mazda motor corp of hiroshima, japan. it gave no financial details. bbc said the supercharger enables diesel engine cars to match the performance of petrol engine vehicles without losing the fuel economy and emission advantages of diesel. mazda is the first international carmaker to use the supercharger in mass production of diesel engines. reuter 
