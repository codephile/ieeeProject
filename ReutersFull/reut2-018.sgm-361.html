westpac banking corp said it is cutting its indicator lending rate to 22 pct from 23 pct effective from june 15. westpac said in a statement various other lending rates are also being revised downwards. the westpac move follows base rate drops by other new zealand trading banks recently. base rate drops to 22 pct from 23 on june 16 and rate falls to 21.50 pct from 23 on june 15. the australia and new zealand banking group ltd rate remains at 23 pct. reuter 
