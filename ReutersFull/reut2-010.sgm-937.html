martin marietta corp said it signed an exclusive agreement with mcdonnell douglas corp's tymnet co for tymnet to provide data communications services as a member of its team bidding on the federal government's fts 2000 telecommunications system. tymnet is a unit of mcdonnell's information systems group. the government's fts 2000 system will replace the current one operated for federal agencies by the general services administration. the gsa issued a request for proposals in january, with responses due in june and selection of a contractor scheduled by year end. reuter 
