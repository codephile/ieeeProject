the first malaysian investment fund for foreign investors will be launched in the united states market next month to raise between 60 to 69 mln dlrs, (ambb) said. the fund, called , will be jointly sponsored by the merchant bank and the international fund corporation of the world bank, said ambb in a statement. the two institutions, together with merrill lynch capital markets and morgan stanley inc , are underwriters for the fund, it added. ambb said its wholly-owned subsidiary will be the fund's malaysian investment adviser while will manage it. it added that 80 pct of the money raised will be used to invest in shares of malaysian companies on the kuala lumpur stock exchange and the remaining in unlisted shares. ambb said the fund will expose the malaysian market to us and japanese investors and help develop the local capital market. reuter 
