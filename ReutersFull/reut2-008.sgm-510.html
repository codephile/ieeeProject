on thursday some 1,350 inspectors will check whether merchants in yugoslavia have fully complied with government orders to roll back prices, the official tanjug news agency said. the government of prime minister branko mikulic last friday ordered the roll back of prices to the end of 1986 level on all products the cost of which has increased over 20.3 pct in the first two months of 1987, as the country's inflation soared towards 100 pct. tanjug said over 19,000 products will be affected. it said that the price of some 4,933 industrial food products has gone up in january an february from 104 to 775 pct. reuter 
