swedish mining and metals group boliden ab said the takeover of the u.s. allis-chalmers corp's mining machinery division made it the world's leading maker of such equipment. president kjell nilsson, announcing the 600 mln crown deal, told a news conference boliden would now become a truly international concern with operations in brazil, chile and other big minerals-producing nations. he said the allis-chalmers' division, accounting for some 50 pct of the u.s. group's sales, would fit in well into boliden. reuter 
