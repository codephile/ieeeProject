ibm australia credit ltd is issuing a 75 mln australian dlr eurobond due april 24, 1990 with a 14-1/8 pct coupon and priced at 101-1/4, salomon brothers international ltd said as lead manager. the non-callable bonds will be issued in denominations of 1,000 and 10,000 dlrs and will be listed in luxembourg. gross fees of 1-1/2 pct comprise 1/2 pct for management and underwriting combined and one pct for selling. there will be co-leads. the borrower is wholly owned by international business machines corp . reuter 
