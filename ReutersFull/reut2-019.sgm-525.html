the republic of portugal is issuing a 700 mln french franc variable-rate bond due july 1995 at 100.05 pct, lead manager credit commercial de france said. the issue of unsecured, unsubordinated debt will have a coupon based on three-month libor plus 20 basis points. if on any interest date this is 25 pct or more above three-month paris interbank offered rates then the interest rate will be three-month pibor plus 30 basis points. payment date is july 24. there is a call at par at the end of the first year and thereafter on any interest payment date. listing is paris and denominations are of 10,000 and 100,000 francs. fees total 50 basis points, with 25 points for selling and 25 for management and underwriting combined including a six basis points praecipuum. the issue is rated a by moody's. reuter 
