metropolitan consolidated industries inc said it will make a tender offer for all or any of the outstanding shares of common stock of metex corp not currently held by or under option to metropolitan for 7.75 dlrs per share. the company said the offer will be subject ot securing satisfactory financing. metropolitan currently owns about 25.7 pct of metex common and holds options to purchase about an additional 16 pct of metex common stock. metropolitan said the offer will be made no later than october 27. reuter 
