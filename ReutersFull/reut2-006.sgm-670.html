publicker industries inc said its will receive an unqualified opinion on 1986 financial statements from auditor arthur andersen and co and the opinion on its 1985 statements will be changed to unqualified from qualified. the company said the 1985 opinion had been qualified due to the uncertainty of the ultimate amount recoverable from the assets of its british beverage division. it said it has been in the process of substantially reducing its inventory of scotch whisky and intends to complete the reduction by the end of 1987. it said it expects to recover its 12 mln dlr investment in the division by the end of the year. reuter 
