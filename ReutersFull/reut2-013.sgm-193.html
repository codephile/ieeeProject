michael krupp, a golden, colo., businessman and major shareholder of kappa networks inc, said he and kappa's management are considering seeking a merger between kappa and radiation systems inc. in a filing with the securities and exchange commission, krupp said companies he controls and kappa acquired a combined 292,000 radiation system shares, or 5.25 pct of the total. krupp, who has a 24.4 pct stake in kappa, said he and kappa management believe a kappa-radiation systems combination would be good for both companies. but no decision has been made on whether or how to proceed with a merger attempt, he added. reuter 
