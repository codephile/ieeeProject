amr corp's american airlines and citibank are expected to announce a joint marketing program at a press conference tomorrow, analysts said. the program will probably allow consumers using a credit card issued by citibank to pay for flights on american and earn frequent flyer miles, they said. "it's an incentive to use the card and it potentially builds traffic for the carrier," said one analyst, who asked not to be named. one analyst noted that texas air corp's continental airlines has a similar agreement with a bank issuing mastercard. texas air officials were not immediately available. american airlines and citibank scheduled a press conference at 1030 est in new york tomorrow to announce a "major new credit card alliance marketing program." spokesmen for citibank declined additional comment. american airlines spokesmen were not available. reuter 
