ncr corp said it cut prices on its personal computers, the 80286-based pc8 and 8088-based pc6 models, by 10 pct to 21 pct effective immediately, to maintain a competitive position on pricing. ncr said prices of its high-end pc8 models have been cut by 10 pct to 13 pct and by 10 pct in its pc8 model designed to compete with 286-based xt-level pcs. it also reduced the list price of its pc6 model configured with 256 ram and 360 k flex drive, along with other products in the line. ncr also said it reduced the price of its 64mb fixed disk drive to 2,995 dlrs from 3,595 dlrs. reuter 
