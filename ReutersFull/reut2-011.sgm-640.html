concord fabrics inc said it has restructered its long-term debt with an institutional lender. the company said it extended its debt by borrowing 15 mln dlrs and paying off its existing term loan of 3,100,000 dlrs, resulting in a new debt of 11.9 mln dlrs. the company said the remaining funds will be used to equip a prooduction facility in chino, calif., and provide working capital. reuter 
