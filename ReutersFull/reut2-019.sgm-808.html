crown crafts inc said it expects to report record sales and earnings for the june 28 first quarter. the company said its prior record sales quarter was the second quarter of fiscal 1987 with sales of 15,097,000 dlrs. its prior record net earnings and primary earnings per share of 877,000 dlrs, or 1.15 dlrs a share, were achieved in the third quarter of fiscal 1987. crown also said its backlog stands at more than 17.0 mln dlrs, double the backlog a year ago. reuter 
