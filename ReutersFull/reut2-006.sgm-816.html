national data communications inc said it is no longer obligated to issue its majority shareholder 20 mln common shares under a previous agreement. national said it entered into a new agreement with signature, which would have owned about 80 pct of national's common under the prior pact. under the new pact, national said it granted signature a five-year option to acquire two mln shares of its common for an option price equal to its current market value. national has about 15.3 mln shares outstanding. reuter 
