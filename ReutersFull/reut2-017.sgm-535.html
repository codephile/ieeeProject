mercedes-benz credit corp is issuing a 100 mln dlr eurobond due june 25, 1992 paying 8-1/4 pct and priced at 100-1/2 pct, lead manager deutsche bank capital markets said. the non-callable bond is available in denominations of 1,000 and 10,000 dlrs and will be listed in luxembourg. the selling concession is 1-1/4 pct while management and underwriting combined pays 5/8 pct. the payment date is june 25. reuter 
