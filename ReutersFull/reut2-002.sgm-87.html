nissan motor co ltd is issuing a 35 billion yen eurobond due march 25 1992 paying 5-1/8 pct and priced at 103-3/8, nikko securities co (europe) ltd said. the non-callable issue is available in denominations of one mln yen and will be listed in luxembourg. the payment date is march 25. the selling concession is 1-1/4 pct while management and underwriting combined pays 5/8 pct. nikko said it was still completing the syndicate. reuter 
