credit national is issuing a 100 mln dlr eurobond, due april 23, 1992 with a 7-3/8 pct coupon and priced at 101-5/8 pct, ltcb international said as lead manager. the bonds, guaranteed by the french government, will be issued in denominations of 5,000 dlrs and will be listed in luxembourg. gross fees of 1-7/8 pct comprise 5/8 pct for management and underwriting combined and 1-1/4 pct for selling. reuter 
