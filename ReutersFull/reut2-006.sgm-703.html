navistar international corp said it and dina camiones, a truck subsidiary of diesel nacional, a mexican government company, said they signed a technical assistance agreement. it said the agreement provides for navistar to provide technical knowledge and assistance to dina camiones for the production of international s-series medium and heavy trucks for sale and distribution in mexico, central and south america. it said production of the ne wmodels by dina is expected to begin in january 1988. reuter 
