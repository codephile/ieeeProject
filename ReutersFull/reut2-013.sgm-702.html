rent-a-center inc is raising 35 mln dlrs via an offering of convertible subordinated debentures due 2012 with a 5-3/4 pct coupon and par pricing, said sole manager morgan stanley and co inc. the debentures are convertible into the company's common stock at 40.50 dlrs per share, representing a premium of 25.58 pct over the stock price when terms on the debt were set. non-callable for two years, the issue is rated ba-3 by moody's investors service inc and bb-minus by standard and poor's corp. reuter 
