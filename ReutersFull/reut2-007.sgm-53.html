the two top officials of the chicago board of trade, cbt, are planning to visit three countries in the far east next month to drum up support for the exchange's proposed night trading session, cbt president thomas donovan told reuters. donovan said he and cbt chairman karsten mahlmann will leave april 3 and spend two weeks in hong kong, tokyo and sydney discussing the night trading session with brokerage firms, regulators and exchanges. cbt hopes to launch the first u.s. night trading session at the end of april, offering treasury-bond and t-note futures and options on the two futures contracts between 1700 and 2100 hrs central u.s. time (2300 to 0300 hrs gmt). reuter 
