kawasaki heavy industries ltd will increase output of motorcycles, dune buggies and jet skis to 380,000 units in the year starting april 1 from 303,000 a year earlier, a company spokesman said. exports in 1987/88 are forecast to jump to 335,000 from 271,000 a year earlier, while domestic sales will rise to around 45,000 from 32,000, he told reuters. of these, exports to the u.s. will rise to 150,000 from 120,000 a year earlier. the company also plans to lift motorcycle production at its wholly owned u.s. subsidiary to 1985/86 levels of 75,000 from 67,000 in 1986/87. reuter 
