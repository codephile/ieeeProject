dutch seasonally adjusted industrial production fell by 6.4 pct in april compared with the previous month, and was 5.5 pct down on the same period last year, figures from the official statistics bureau cbs show. the april index, base 1980, stood at 103. in march, industrial production rose by 2.8 pct from february. the unadjusted april index figure fell 14 pct from march to 104, compared with a 10 pct rise in march over february. reuter 
