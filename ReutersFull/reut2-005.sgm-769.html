mitsubishi bank ltd's hong kong branch is planning a 40 mln new zealand dlr certificate of deposit, cd, issue, banking sources said. the three-year cds are denominated in 100,000 dlr units and will carry interest at 37.5 basis points below the three-month new zealand bank bill rate. payment date is expected to be around april 1. anz securities asia ltd is lead manager for the issue. reuter 
