finnish export corp is issuing a 20 billion yen bond maturing april 23, 1992 carrying a 4-3/8 pct coupon and priced at 102-3/4, said yamaichi securities international (europe) ltd. the bond are payable april 23, 1987 and are available in denominations of one mln yen. they will be listed on the london stock exchange. there is a 1-1/4 pct selling concession and 5/8 pct combined management and underwriting fee. reuter 
