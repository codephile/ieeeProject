the yugoslav power industry yesterday floated its second issue of domestic bonds in three months, after an experimental issue in january, official sources said. associated electric power enterprises in the yugoslav republic of serbia issued the bonds to raise eight billion dinars for the construction of new power facilities. the sources said the bonds mature in eight months with 55 pct interest but no further details were available. january's bonds were oversubscribed as their interest rate was two points above savings deposit rates and were the first bonds that could be exchanged or converted before maturity. reuter 
