financial holding company nord-est is issuing a 392.9 mln franc, 6.25 pct, five year domestic bond convertible into shares, an announcement in the official bulletin (balo) said. from march 26 to april 8, subscription for 1.63 mln of the 1.64 mln bonds of 240 francs nominal each will be reserved for shareholders holding 13.09 mln shares of 50 francs nominal, representing the company's capital of 654.85 mln francs, on the basis of one bond for eight shares held. the bonds will be exchangeable from july 1 for shares on a one for one basis. reuter 
