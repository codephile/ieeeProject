hertz realty corp is issuing a 75 mln canadian dlr bond due april 30, 1993 paying nine pct and priced at 100-3/4 pct, syndicate sources said. the non-callable bond is guaranteed by hertz corp. it is available in denominations of 1,000 and 10,000 canadian dlrs and will be listed in luxembourg. the selling concession is 1-1/4 pct while management and underwriting combined pays 5/8 pct. the payment date is april 30. reuter 
