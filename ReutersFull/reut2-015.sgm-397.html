mitsubishi corp said it will issue a 50 billion yen unsecured seven-year convertible bond and a 30 billion yen unsecured nine-year convertible bond on the domestic capital market through public placement with nikko securities co ltd as lead manager. coupon and conversion price for both par-priced bonds will be set at a board meeting to be held later this month and payment is due on may 14, mitsubishi said in a statement. the seven-year bond matures on september 30, 1994, and the nine-year bond on september 30, 1996. mitsubishi's share price fell 30 yen to 1,200 on the tokyo stock exchange today. reuter 
