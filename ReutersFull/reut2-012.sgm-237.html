quaker oats co said its stokely-van camp inc subsidiary will retire on may 15 its 10-1/4 pct sinking fund debentures due may 15, 2000. the company said it will retire 2.6 mln dlrs at par through operation of the debenture's sinking fund. the remaining outstanding balance, currently 11,812,000 dlrs, will be retired at a price of 1,041 dlrs a bond. interest will be payable may 15, record may one. reuter 
