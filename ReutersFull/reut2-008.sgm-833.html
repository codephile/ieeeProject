hercules inc said it named david hollingsworth as chairman and chief executive, succeeding alexander giacco who is retiring effective april 1. hercules also said it named fred buckner as president and chief operating officer and arden engebretsen as vice chairman and chief financial officer. the company also said it expects its new product, synpulp, to grow into a 100 mln dlr a year business over the next five years. metton, another new product, is expected to yield 300-500 mln dlrs a year by the late 1990s. reuter 
