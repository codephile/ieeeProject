the convertible eurobond issue announced yesterday for genentech inc has been increased to a total of 150 mln dlrs from 100 mln, credit suisse first boston ltd (csfb) said as lead manager. the coupon has been set at five pct and conversion price at 74 dlrs per share which represents a 23.85 pct premium over genentech's stock closing level of 59.75 on the new york stock exchange last night. payment date has been brought forward to march 27 from march 30 and the bonds will mature on march 27, 2002. reuter 
