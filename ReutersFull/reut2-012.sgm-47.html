equicor, equitable hca corp, said that the company will likely attain revenues in excess of two billion dlrs in its first year of operations. the company, created last october with initial equity of 400 mln dlrs, is owned equally by the equitable life assurance society of the u.s. and hospital corp of america . financial results for the first six months of the company's operations were not disclosed. equicor provides employee group plans to 1,500 corporations nationwide. it said it aims to double its marketshare in five years from the about 3.5 pct of the employee benefits industry it controls. reuter 
