the north carolina department of commerce said electrolux ab of sweden's white consolidated industries subsidiary will locate a new 15 mln dlr 352,000 square foot national appliance parts distribution center at asheville, n.c. it said the center will handle parts for frigidaire, tappan, kelvinator, gibson, vesta and white-westinghouse product lines. each now has a separate distribution center. the department said construction will start immediately and should be finished in late 1987. reuter 
