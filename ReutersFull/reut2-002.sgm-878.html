the united states plans to sell 84 mln dlrs in electronic equipment to south korea to upgrade that country's hawk anti-aircraft missiles, the reagan administration announced. the sale of 28 modification kits to upgrade the i-hawk (improved hawk) missiles already in south korea is expected to become official in 30 days without objection from congress. prime contractor on the sale is the raytheon corp , which builds the hawk medium-range missiles. reuter 
