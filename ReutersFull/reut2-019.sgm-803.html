control data corp said the aeronautical systems division of the u.s. air force awarded it a 13.7 mln dlr contract to provide a central datacomm system. it said the system will act as the central interface between numerous user organizations and the division's information systems and technology center at wright-patterson air force base in ohio. terms of the five-year, two-phase contract call for control data to supply equipment, software, maintenance, training and support services. reuter 
