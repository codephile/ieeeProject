the u.s. agriculture department said it has accepted a bid for an export bonus to cover the sale of 18,000 tonnes of u.s. durum wheat to algeria. a bonus of 43.25 dlrs per tonne was awarded to cam usa inc on the shipment scheduled for june 20-30, melvin sims, usda general sales manager, said. an additional 228,000 tonnes of durum wheat are available to algeria under the department's export enhancement program, sims said. reuter 
