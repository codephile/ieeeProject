rainier bancorp said it completed the acquisition of tacoma, wash.-based united bank, for 59 mln dlrs worth of rainier stock. united, a savings bank with 607 mln dlrs in assets and 23 offices, will operate as a wholly-owned subsidiary, rainier said. rainier, which last month agreed to merge with security pacific corp , said the united acquisition will increase its assets to 9.8 billion dlrs from the 9.2 billion reported at the end of 1986. reuter 
