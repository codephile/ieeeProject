winley home builders inc said it completed a land purchase agreement with m.d.c. holdings inc for 1,090,000 dlrs in notes and preferred stock. the company said it bought 33 lots in colorado from richmond homes ltd, an m.d.c. unit, in return for 650,000 dlrs in promissory notes and 440,000 dlrs in preferred stock. the company said m.d.c. has an option to convert winley preferred stock into 20 pct winley common in a year. reuter 
