reef energy corp said its board entered into agreements with penteco corp, a private tulsa-based company, to buy a 12-pct interest in the general partnership of penteco east central pipeline and a 10-pct interest in lincoln gas and marketing corp. penteco east is a gas gathering and transmission system in southern kansas and northern oklahoma. it said penteco in turn has purchased one mln shares of reef common and taken options for the purchase of another two mln shares over the next 36 months. reuter 
