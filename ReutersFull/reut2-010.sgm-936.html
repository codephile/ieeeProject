gac liquidating trust said it has completed the sale of its rio rico 16,653 acre undeveloped land tract in nogales, ariz., for about 4,725,000 dlrs. it said it received about one mln dlrs in cash at closing, less costs and expenses, and a 3,725,000 dlr promissory note secured by irrevocable bank letters of credit and a first deed of trust on substantially all the property sold. the promissory note is payable in cash without interest one year after closing. reuter 
