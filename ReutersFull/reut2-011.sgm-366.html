chrysler corp said it is extending its low annual percentage rate (apr) financing, sales-incentive program on some 1986 and 1987 model cars and trucks until april 10 from march 31. chrysler said the program offers customers the choice between chrysler's low apr financing of 3.7 pct for 24 months, 5.9 pct for 36 months, 6.9 pct for 48 months or 9.9 pct for 60 months and a cash incentive, or a combination of low rate financing through chrysler credit corp plus a cash incentive. reuter 
