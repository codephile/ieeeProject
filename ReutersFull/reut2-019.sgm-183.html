computer products inc is raising 35 mln dlrs through an offering of convertible subordinated debentures due 1997 with a 9-1/2 pct coupon and par pricing, said sole manager robinson-humphrey co inc. the debentures are convertible into the company's common stock at 4.625 dlrs per share, representing a premium of 23.33 pct over the stock price when terms on the debt were set. non-callable for three years, the issue is rated b-3 by moody's and ccc-minus by standard and poor's. the gross spread is 38.75 dlrs, the selling concession is 20 dlrs and the reallowance is 10 dlrs. reuter 
