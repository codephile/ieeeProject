the european investment bank said it is making a 300 mln franc loan to peugeot sa to restructure the paint works at its sochaux car factory in france. it said the ten-year loan carries interest at 9.15 pct and follows a 300 mln franc 8.60 pct ten-year loan the bank made to peugeot last july. work on the paint shop at sochaux is part of a 1.3 billion franc operation to modernise the group's factories, it added. reuter 
