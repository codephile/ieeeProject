u.k. property company mepc plc is issuing a 75 mln stg eurobond due april 15, 2004 paying 9-7/8 pct and priced at 99-5/8 pct, lead manager county natwest capital markets said. the bond is in partly paid form with 25 pct due on april 15 and the remainder on july 15. it will be available in denominations of 1,000 and 10,000 stg and will be listed in london. fees comprise 1-1/2 pct selling concession and 1/2 pct each for management and underwriting. reuter 
