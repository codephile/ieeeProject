canon inc said it will stop making japanese language word processors with retail prices below 50,000 yen and consign production instead to , a 51.75-pct owned unit of matsushita electric industrial co ltd . a canon spokesman said the move reflects intense price competition as canon, casio computer co ltd and others compete in the growing market for low-end processors. canon will focus production on more expensive and sophisticated models which have more generous profit margins, he said. reuter 
