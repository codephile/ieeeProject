a textile mill with an annual production capacity of 52 mln square metres of knitted fabric has been opened officially near havana, granma, the official cuban newspaper, said. the mill, inaugurated yesterday, is equipped with looms and other machinery from east germany. a single production line, in operation since last year, has already produced over seven mln square metres of fabric. president fidel castro and vice-president carlos rafael rodriguez were present at the ceremony. reuter 
