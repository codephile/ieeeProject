eldon industries inc expects a record july 12 second quarter, chairman robert silverstein told the new york society of security analysts. the company's previous best second quarter was the period ended july 12, 1986 when eldon had earnings of 1,353,000 dlrs, or 31 cts a share on sales of 20.7 mln dlrs. silverstein said, "if these positive trends continue, we look forward to favorable quarterly comparisons for the balance of the year, resulting in another record year in sales and income." reuter 
