the amount of a convertible eurobond launched on friday for metal box plc has been increased to 65 mln stg from the original 60 mln and the coupon has been fixed at 5-3/4 pct, swiss bank corp international ltd said as lead manager. the conversion price has been fixed at 262p, which represents a premium of 9.62 pct over friday's closing share price of 239p. the indicated coupon on friday was 5-3/4 to six pct. reuter 
