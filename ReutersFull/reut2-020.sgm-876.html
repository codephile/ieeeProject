cityfed financial corp said it will from time to time make open market purchases of its common stock for an amount not to exceed one mln dlrs. the shares will be held by the company as treasury stock. cityfed also said that its city federal savings bank unit will be acquiring additional shares for the units employee stock ownership trust. the additional purchase by the employee stock trust will be funded by a loan up to five mln dlrs. at the current stock price of 5.50 dlr to 6.50 dlrs, cityfed and the trust will be able to purchase about 950,000 additional shares, or about five pct of shares outstanding. reuter 
