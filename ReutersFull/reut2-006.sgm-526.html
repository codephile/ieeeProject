exovir inc said it sold to an investor group led by mark hammer 200,000 shares of company common stock and warrants to purchase an additional 200,000 shares. exovir said the transaction increases the group's stake from 12.9 pct to 18.5 pct. the net proceeds to the company total 2.8 mln dlrs, the company said. the warrants are exerciseable over the next three years at 19.50 dlrs per share of common stock, according to exovir. reuter 
