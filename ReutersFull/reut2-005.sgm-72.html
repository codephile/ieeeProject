general electric co said it will redeem on may 1 rca corp's 75 mln dlrs of 11-1/2 pct notes due 1990. general electric is the successor obligator to rca. ge said it will buy back the notes at par plus accrued interest to, but not including, may 1. no further interest will accrue on the notes after the redemption date. bankers trust co is trustee. reuter 
