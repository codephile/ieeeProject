goldman, sachs and co told the securities and exchange commission it sold nearly its entire stake in first pennsylvania corp of 2,450,598 shares, or 5.2 pct of the total outstanding common stock. goldman sachs said it has about 17,800 first pennsylvania common shares remaining. on february 26, the company redeemed convertible debentures with common stock, which at once increased goldman's stock holding and the total outstanding. goldman sachs said it originally bought the stake as part of its arbitrage business. marine midland banks has an agreement in principal to acquire first pennsylvania. reuter 
