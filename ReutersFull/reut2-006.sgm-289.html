firstcorp inc said its board has increased the maximum number of class a shares in its repurchase program to 250,000 from 200,000 shares. the program was first adopted in june 1986 and provided for a maximum of 100,000 shares to be repurchased through june 1987. in june 1986, the company increased the shares to 200,000 and extended the period to end september 1987. reuter 
