salomon brothers inc announced formation of global stock indexes designed to make it easy for portfolio money managers to invest all over the world. "this is a way to put money into the pacific or europe quickly," laszlo birinyi, salomon vice president laszlo birinyi told a news conference. the stock index products were developed in cooperation with the frank russell co, a pension fund consulting firm. birinyi said he would not be surprised if exchanges create futures and options on the indexes. "somebody will - if they don't, we will," he said. reuter 
