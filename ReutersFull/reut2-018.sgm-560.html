usx corp announced a public offering of 250 mln dlrs of convertible exchangeable cumulative preference stock underwritten by morgan stanley and co inc and first boston corp. usx said the proceeds will be used to redeem its currently outstanding 2.25 dlrs convertible exchangeable cumulative preference stock. usx said the new stock will be offered at 50 dlrs a share and will have a dividend of 3.50 dlrs a share per year. reuter 
