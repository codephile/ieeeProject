marcor development co inc said it expects to post a profit for its fiscal year ended february 28 of about two mln dlrs, compared to a year earlier loss of 3.2 mln dlrs. during the prior year the company operated as f and m importing, a publicly-held wholesale food distributor. during march last year privately-held marcor acquired a controlling interest in f and m importing, divested the food business, renamed the company and began operating as a real estate investment and service company. reuter 
