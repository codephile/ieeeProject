nissan motor co ltd said it has started to market a remodelled version of its four wheel drive (4wd) safari vehicle in japan. nissan said in a statement it hopes to sell 250 vehicles a month in japan. it also plans soon to start exporting 3,400 vehicles a month to the australian, middle east and asian markets under the name patrol, a spokesman said. it will sell the vehicle in europe sometime in the future, with shipments from , its spanish unit. the volume for europe will be set later. reuter 
