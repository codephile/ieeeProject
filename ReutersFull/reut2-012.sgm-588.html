the stock of international business machine corp declined slightly today and shares of digital equipment corp rose after brokerage house morgan stanley and co reiterated a negative opinion on ibm and a buy on digital, traders said. ibm, which this morning introduced four new personal computers, the first major overhaul of its pc line since it entered the business in 1981, fell 1-3/4 to 149-3/8. digital equipment rose 1-3/8 to 163-5/8. analyst carol muratore of morgan stanley was unavailable for comment. 
