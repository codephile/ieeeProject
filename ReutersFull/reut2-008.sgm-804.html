salomon brothers inc, acting as sole underwriter, said it is publicly offering 50,000 units of van dusen airport services co, l.p. the offering consists of 50 mln dlrs of 12 pct senior subordinated notes due 1997 and 50,000 contingent payment obligations. the notes are priced at par, or 1,000 dlrs each. a unit has one par value note and one contingent payment obligation. the notes are redeemable by van dusen after march 15, 1992, salomon said. a payment obligation entitles the holder to receive a cash payment under certain events, or to certain repurchase rights. reuter 
