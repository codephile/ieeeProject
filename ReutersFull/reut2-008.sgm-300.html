cancapital corp said it sold four mln common shares and one mln common share purchase warrants of to merrill lynch canada inc and first marathon securities ltd for resale to public investors. the common shares will be resold at 15.875 dlrs per share and the warrants will be resold at 7.25 dlrs per warrant. each warrant may be converted into one common shares of poco at 9.50 dlrs per share through january 15, 1989. gross proceeds of 70.8 mln dlrs will be added to the working capital cancapital corp. reuter 
