textron inc's avco lycoming textron subsidiary said it finalized a 1.4 billion dlr five-year contract with the u.s. army for m1 abrams main battle tank gas turbine engines. under the contract, avco said the army will procure 3,299 installed engines plus spare engines and a combination of modules and gear boxes. it also said the agreement includes an initial purchase of 173 engines by the u.s. marines under a program that will result in a fleet of 560 m1 tanks. reuter 
