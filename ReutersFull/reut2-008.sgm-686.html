schleswig-holstein landesbank (lb) finance bv is issuing a 30 mln australian dlr bond due april 30, 1990 carrying a coupon of 14-5/8 pct and priced at 101-3/8, said anz merchant bank ltd as lead manager. the securities are guaranteed by parent company, schleswig-holstein landesbank girozentrale and will be listed on the luxembourg stock exchange. they are available in denominations of 1,000 dlrs. payment date is april 30. there is a one pct selling concession and a 1/2 pct combined underwriting and management fee. reuter 
