commodore international corp said it entered into two agreements to provide amiga computer graphics technology to the coin-operated amusement industry. the agreements are with london-based and of elk grove, ill., and they join a similar previously-announced agreement with bally manufacturing corp , the company said. under the terms of the agreement, commodore said it will supply the companies with amiga printed circuit board and commodore technical knowhow and will obtain software licensing rights to video games developed for the amiga hardware. 
