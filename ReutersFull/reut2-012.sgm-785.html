the indian state trading corporation (stc) bought 20,000 tonnes of optional origin soybean oil and 6,000 tonnes of rbd palm olein at its import tender yesterday, traders said. pakistan, however, rejected offers at its tender for 12,000 tonnes of rbd palm oil, but is expected to reenter the market next week, they said. the stc soyoil purchase was for may 20/jun 20 shipment at 319 dlrs per tonne cif and the palm olein for apr 25/may 25 shipment at 355 dlrs per tonne. reuter 
