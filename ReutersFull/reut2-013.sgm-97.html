chrysler canada ltd, wholly owned by chrysler corp, said it sold 16,484 passenger cars in march, a 9.7 pct increase over the 15,024 sold in the same month of last year. the company said truck sales rose 32 pct to a march record of 7,400 units from 5,603 in march of last year. for the first quarter, car sales totaled 34,578 compared with 37,097 a year earlier, while truck sales totaled 15,397 compared with 14,069. reuter 
