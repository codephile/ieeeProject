security federal savings and loan association said the initial public offering of its holding company security financial group inc has been completed. the company said the offering by security financial consisted of 766,251 issued through the conversion of security federal to stock ownership from mutual ownership. it said net proceeds of the offering will be used for general corporate purposes. trident securities inc and dain bosworth inc were underwriters for the offering. security financial will begin trading on nasdaq on or about june 29, the company said. reuter 
