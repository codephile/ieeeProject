imre corp said it completed a private placement of about 400,000 shares of its securities with a group of european institutions for 2.5 mln dlrs. the company said proceeds will be used for working capital and to implement the company's business plan. imre also said it has established a light manufacturing facility in richmond, washington that makes raw material components for prosorba column, a medical device that removes disease-related immune components from the human bloodstream. the company is awaiting food and drug administration approval on the device. reuter 
