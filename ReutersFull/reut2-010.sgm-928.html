mcdonald's corp said it is offering to buy the 25 mln dlrs in its outstanding 10-7/8 pct debentures due july 15, 2015. it said debentures will be purchased at 1,118.30 dlrs per 1,000 dlrs principal amount, plus accrued interest. the original debenture issue was for 100 mln dlrs. it said the buyback offer expires april eight, unless extended. reuter 
