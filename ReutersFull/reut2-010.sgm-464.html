canadian wholesale merchant sales rose 8.9 pct in january over the same month in 1986 with all but one of the major trade groups posting increases, statistics canada said. in december sales rose 13.7 pct. the federal agency said wholesalers of tobacco, drugs and toilet preparations posted a 0.9 pct decline in sales. inventories were 5.3 pct higher than in january 1986. reuter 
