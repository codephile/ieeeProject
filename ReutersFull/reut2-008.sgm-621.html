american can co is issuing a 150 mln dlr convertible eurobond due april 22, 2002 carrying an indicated coupon of 5-1/2 to 5-7/8 pct priced at par, lead manager morgan stanley international said. terms will be set this week with a conversion premium of 25 to 28 pct. the bond is available in denominations of 1,000 and 10,000 dlrs and be listed in luxembourg. there is no call for three years, subsequently at par plus the coupon, declining by equal amounts per annum until 1997. fees comprise 1-1/2 pct selling concession and one pct management and underwriting combined. reuter 
