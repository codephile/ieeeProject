amoco corp said its weqb-1 exploratory well 39 miles east of galeota point on trinidad's east coast flowed 24 mln cubic feet of natural gas and 500 barrels of condensate daily from one zone and 29 mln cubic feet of natural gas and 600 barrels of condensate daily from a second. the company said both flows were through 40/64 inch chokes and from zones between 10,000 and 13,000 feet in depth. the well, in 260 feet of water, was drilled to a total depth of 14,629 feet, it said. it said the discovery was made in sands previously untested in the area. reuter 
