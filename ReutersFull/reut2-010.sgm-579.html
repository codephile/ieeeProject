salomon sa, the french maker of ski bindings and boots, said it is seeking a 75 mln dlr euro-commercial paper program that will be supported by a 75 mln dlr committed syndicated revolving credit. the launch of the program is still subject to final approval of the french authorities. morgan guaranty ltd is the arranger for the program and the revolving credit and will act as one of the dealers for the commercial paper along with credit suisse first boston ltd. morgan guaranty trust co of new york, london branch, will be issuing and paying agent. none of the terms were disclosed. reuter 
