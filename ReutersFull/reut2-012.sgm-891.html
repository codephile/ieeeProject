volkswagen of america inc, the u.s. arm of volkswagen ag, said its domestic car sales for late march fell to 2,145 vehicles, from 3,342 vehicles for the same period in 1986. volkswagen added that for the month of march it sold 4,324 vehicles, down from 6,198 last year. for the year-to-date, volkswagen said it sold 10,951 vehicles, versus 18,392 last year-to-date. reuter 
