synergen inc said it agreed to provide its human elastase inhibitor, designed to treat emphysema, to ciba-geigy ag for human clinical trials. ciba-geigy is currently testing the substance in animals, but the company said the new supplies of the drug will be suitable for human tests. the company said ciba-geigy initially licensed the product in 1985. the new agreement calls for synergen to set up a commercial manufacturing process to produce the drug and deliver quantities to ciba-geigy in the second half 1987. reuter 
