borden inc said it called for redemption on may one its outstanding 605,000 dlrs of 4-3/8 pct sinking fund debentures due 1991. it will buy back the debentures at par plus accrued interest of 18.23 dlrs for each 1,000 dlr debenture. the trustee is chase manhattan bank n.a. and the paying agency is chemical bank, the company said. reuter 
