standard and poor's corp said it raised to bbb-plus from bbb hechinger co's 86 mln dlrs of convertible subordinated debentures of 2009. s and p cited hechinger's outstanding record of sales and earnings growth as well as what it termed the company's position as one of the best operators in the retail home improvement industry. it also said the firm's financial performance as measured by profitability and debt leverage underscore strong credit quality. s and p assigned a bbb-plus rating to hechinger's planned 100 mln dlr issue of convertible debt due 2012. reuter 
