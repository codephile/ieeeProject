cns inc said it completed its initial public offering of 1,035,000 shares of common stock at 3.75 dlrs a share through an underwriting group headed by engler-budd and co inc. concurrent with the offering, the company made an exchange offer in which it issued 277,127 common shares in exchange for 935,333 dlrs of outstanding promissory notes at the rate of 3.375 dlrs a share. this represented over a 93 pct acceptance of the exchange offer, the company said. reuter 
