ual inc's united airlines said it will offer scheduled service between burbank, calif., and denver, colo., starting june five. united said it will operate two daily round-trip flights in the burbank to denver market using new boeing co 737-300 aircraft. united said as a promotion for its new route it will offer a bonus of double mileage to all members of its frequent flyer prgram, mileage plus, who fly the burbank-denver route during the first 45 days of service. reuter 
