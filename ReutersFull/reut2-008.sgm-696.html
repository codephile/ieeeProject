british services company bet plc said its shareholders had given it approval to raise up to 170 mln dlrs through a share issue to u.s. investors. a company spokesman told reuters the issue of up to 44 mln shares will be in the form of american depository receipts (adrs) and that the total amount to be raised will probably be 75 to 100 mln dlrs, depending on market conditions. the issue is due to be completed in august, he said. bet shareholders also approved a one-for-one free share issue to existing stockholders, the cancellation of preferred stock and new articles of association, the company said. reuter 
