allied supermarkets inc said it filed with the securities and exchange commission a registration statement covering 240 mln dlrs of debt securities. the company plans to offer 140 mln dlrs of senior subordinated discount debentures and 100 mln dlrs of subordinated debentures. proceeds will be used to finance allied's acquisition of vons cos inc. allied named drexel burnham lambert inc and donaldson, lufkin and jenrette securities corp as co-underwriters of both offerings. reuter 
