ford motor credit co, a unit of ford motor co, is raising 200 mln dlrs via an offering of notes due 1997 yielding 8.13 pct, said sole manager salomon brothers. the notes have an eight pct coupon and were priced at 99.125 to yield 88 basis points over comparable treasuries. that matches the spread over treasuries for the finance unit's equal-sized, same-maturity offering on january 6 that was priced to yield 7.948 pct. this deal was rated a-2 by moody's and aa-minus by standard and poor's. non-callable for five years, today's issue is rated a-1 by moody's and aa-minus by s and p. reuter 
