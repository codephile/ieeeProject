caltex petroleum corp said it will raise posted prices for naphtha and several grades of residual fuel in bahrain, effective march 25. caltex, a joint venture of chevron corp and texaco inc , said its naphtha posting is up four cts a gallon to 43 cts. it said it is raising its marine diesel oil posting by 30 cts a barrel to 20.24 dlrs a barrel. light, medium, and heavy fuel oil postings are up 1.50 dlrs a barrel, the company said. this will bring the light fuel oil price to 16.90 dlrs, medium to 15.50 dlrs, and heavy to 14.60 dlrs, the company said. reuter 
