tri-star pictures inc said it won permanent relief from various provisions of the 1952 consent judgment and 1980 order that apply to the loews' theatre circuit, which tri-star acquired last december. it said the u.s. district court for the southern district of new york agreed to permit exhibition of tri-star motion pictures in loews theaters and to allow tri-star to conduct its business other than with loews free from restructions of the court's earlier orders. reuter 
