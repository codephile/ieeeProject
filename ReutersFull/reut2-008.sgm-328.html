the new york branch of mitsubishi bank ltd said it set a one billion dlr u.s. medium-term certificate of deposit note program. the bank said this will be the largest such program ever established by a u.s. or foreign commercial bank. proceeds of the continuous offering of the medium-term cd notes will be used for general funding purposes of the new york bank. the firm named morgan stanley and co inc as lead agent of the program, with merrill lynch capital markets and salomon brothers inc as co-agents. the parent company had total assets of 210 billion dlrs as of september 30, 1986. reuter 
