the federal deposit insurance corp said state banking regulators closed the morocco state bank in morrocco, indiana, bringing the total number of banks to fail so far this year to 48. the failed bank's 14.1 mln dlrs in deposits and 9.9 mln dlrs of its loans and other assets will be assumed by demotte state bank in demotte, ind, the fdic said. the fdic said it will advance 3.7 mln dlrs to help the transaction and would retain 5.1 mln dlrs in the failed banks assets. reuter 
