the finance ministry said it proposed to the underwriting syndicate a record low 4.7 pct coupon on the 10-year government bond to be issued in april, down from the previous record low five pct march issue. the bond would be priced at 99.75 to yield 4.736 pct compared with 99.50 and 5.075 pct in march, and proposed issue volume is 1,400 billion yen against 475 billion, it said. the syndicate is expected to accept the terms immediately, underwriters said. reuter 
