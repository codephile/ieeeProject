the great atlantic and pacific tea co inc said it withdrew its offer to acquire delchamps inc for 27 dlrs a share or about 175 mln dlrs, which was made earlier this month. michael rourke, a and p vice president, said the company withdrew the offer because delchamps did not respond favorably to it and because of market conditions. he would not comment on whether a and p would make another offer to acquire delchamps. reuter 
