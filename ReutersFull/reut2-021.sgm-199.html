asea ab said the group to be formed by its merger with switzerland's bbc ag brown boveri und cie would have four main business areas; power plants, power transmission, power distribution and industrial equipment. asea brown boveri ltd's international markets would continue to be run by decentralised management, and the three main home markets, switzerland, west germany and sweden, would continue to play an important role, asea said in a statement. it said research and development were crucial to the group's future and central reasearch resources and laboratories in the three home markets would be strengthened. reuter 
