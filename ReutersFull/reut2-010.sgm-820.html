the philippines and the world bank have signed a 300 mln dlr loan and 10 mln dlr worth of technical assistance to help manila in its economic recovery efforts, bank officials said . the loan, to be disbursed in three equal tranches, is repayable over 20 years, including a five-year grace period, with a variable interest rate, currently at 7.92 pct. the loan was signed by finance minister jaime ongpin and a. karasmasoglu, world bank vice-president for east asia and the pacific. ongpin said the first tranche of 100 mln dlrs was expected within a month. reuter 
