a 100 mln dlr euro-commercial paper program has been arranged for svenska finans international bv, manufacturers hanover ltd said as one of two arranger/dealers. the other arranger/dealer is svenska handelsbanken plc. the borrower is the wholly owned international holding company subsidiary of svenska finans ab, a subsidiary of svenska handelsbanken, sweden. in connection with the program, the parent has issued a letter of intent, which is not a guarantee but states the parent's intention to make sure a subsidiary meets its financial obligations. reuter 
