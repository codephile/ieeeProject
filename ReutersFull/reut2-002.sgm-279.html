switzerland's three major banks have offered argentina an extra 110.5 mln swiss franc credit to help finance the building of a heavy water plant there by gebrueder sulzer ag , union bank of switzerland, ubs said. the three -- ubs, credit suisse and swiss bank corp -- provided an initial 409 mln franc credit after argentina signed a contract with sulzer in 1980. the plant will supply its nuclear power industry. the extra credit, provided at market rates, covers extra costs incurred due to building delays, a ubs spokesman said. reuter 
