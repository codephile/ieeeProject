north-rhine westphalia is raising 1.2 billion marks through a 10-year domestic bond with a coupon of 6-1/8 pct and priced at 100.20, lead manager westdeutsche landesbank girozentrale, westlb, said. the bonds yield 6.10 pct at issue, and will be sold from april 2 to 7. payment date is april 17, and the bond matures on the same date in 1997. some one billion marks will be issued for immediate sale, and the remaining 200 mln marks will be set aside for market regulation. listing will be on all eight west german exchanges. reuter 
