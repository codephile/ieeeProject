cabot corp said it called for redemption its outstanding 12-1/4 pct notes due november 1, 1994 and 10-3/4 pct notes due september 1, 1995, aggregating 89 mln dlrs at face amount. it said the redemption is part of a restructuring in which it has said it would divest itself of certain discontinued businesses, repurchase shares and reduce long-term debt. the 12-1/4 pct notes will be redeemed at 108.47 pct plus interest from november 1, 1986. the 10-3/4 pct notes will be redeemed at 108.96 pct plus accrued interest from march 1, 1987. reuter 
