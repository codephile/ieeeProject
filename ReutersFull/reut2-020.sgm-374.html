gander mountain inc said shareholders at its annual meeting approved a doubling of authorized common shares to 10 million from five million for general corporate purposes. the company currently has an average of 2,760,000 common shares outstanding. it reported earnings for the first quarter ended sept 30 of 25 cts a share on net income of 687,000 dlrs, up from 19 cts on 514,00 dlrs in the same 1986 quarter, the company said. reuter 
