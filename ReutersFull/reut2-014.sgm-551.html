the hog/corn ratio for the week ended april 4 was 32.7 versus 32.1 the previous week and 17.1 in the corresponding week a year ago, the u.s. agriculture department said. the steer/corn ratio in the same week was 42.2, versus 41.2 the previous week and 22.9 in the corresponding week a year ago. the ratio represents the number of bushels of corn at omaha that can be bought for 100 lbs of hog or steer. reuter 
