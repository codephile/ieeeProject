volkswagen u.s. inc, a subsidiary of volkswagen ag , said car sales for the february 21 to 28 period fell 42.5 pct to 1,572 from 2,734 cars in the same period last year. there were seven selling days in each period. for the month of february, volkswagen said sales were off 35.2 pct to 3,808 from 5,873 cars in the same month last year. for the year-to-date, u.s. cars sales dropped 45.7 pct to 6,627 from 12,194 cars in the comparable period of 1986, volkswagen said. reuter 
