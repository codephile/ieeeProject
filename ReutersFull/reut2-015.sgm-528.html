rio tinto-zinc corp plc cut its balance sheet gearing, measured as net debt as a proportion of equity, to 35 pct at the end of 1986 from 61 pct a year earlier, finance director andrew buxton told reporters. he said currency movements last year did not greatly affect the earnings outcome, though had a greater impact on the balance sheet. at year-end, 40 pct of its assets were industrial, 35 pct in metal mining and processing and 25 pct in energy. earlier, rtz reported 1986 net attributable profit of 245 mln stg against 257 mln the previous year. reuter 
