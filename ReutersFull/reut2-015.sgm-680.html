amsterdam-rotterdam bank nv said it plans to issue 300 mln 6.75 pct capital bonds due 1988/2007. the bonds will be redeemable in 20 almost equal annual instalments starting june 1, 1988. early redemption is not permitted, the bank said. the issue price will be announced no later than wednesday, april 15. subscriptions close on thursday april 16 at 15.00 hrs local time. the coupon and payment date is june 1. the bonds will be listed on the amsterdam stock exchange. a spokesman for the bank said the capital bonds were subordinated and had an average maturity of 10.5 years. the bonds will be in denominations of 1,000 and 5,000 guilders. reuter 
