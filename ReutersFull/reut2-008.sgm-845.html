k mart corp said its board approved a 17.6 pct increase in the quarterly dividend and declared a three-for-two stock split. the company raised its dividend to 43.5 cts a presplit share, up from the previous 37 cts a share. after the split, the new quarterly dividend rate is equivalent to 29 cts a share. it is payable june eight, record may 21. it said the additional shares will be distributed june five, record may 21. reuter 
