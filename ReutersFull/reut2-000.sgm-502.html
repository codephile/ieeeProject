ncr corp's personal computer division said it signed an agreement to distrubute its personal computer products to 544 stores in the u.s. the company said the agreement covers its entire pc line, which will be sold by computerland franchises, computerland stores inc, and for major corporate bids, through the computerland national accounts program. computerland said it is planning a direct mail campaign and other publicity on its private satellite network to promote the ncr line. the primary products to be sold by computerland stores include the pc8 at-compatible unit, the pc6 dual-speed model and the 2114/pc retail management system, according to ncr. reuter 
