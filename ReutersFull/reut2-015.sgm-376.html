british airways plc is issuing a 100 mln stg eurobond due may 6, 1997, paying 9-1/2 pct and priced at 101-1/2, lead manager union bank of switzerland said. the non-callable bond is available in denominations of 1,000 and 10,000 stg and will be listed in london. the selling concession is 1-1/4 pct while management and underwriting combined pays 3/4 pct. payment date is may 6. reuter 
