genfinance luxembourg is issuing a 50 mln stg eurobond due may 7, 1994 paying 9-1/4 pct and priced at 101-1/4 pct, lead manager kleinwort, benson ltd said. the issue is guaranteed on a subordinated basis by generale bank. it is available in denominations of 1,000 and 10,000 stg and will be listed in luxembourg. the selling concession is 1-1/4 pct while management and underwriting combined pays 5/8 pct. the payment date is may 7. reuter 
