canada plans an auction of three-year bonds march 31 and an auction of seven-year bonds april 1, the finance department said. further details would be announced march 25 on the bonds that will be dated and delivered april 16, 1987. the department said the issues are contingent upon final parliamentary authority being given to bill c-40, a previously announced act to give the government supplementary borrowing authority for fiscal 1987 (march 31) and borrowing authority for fiscal 1988. reuter 
