norway is issuing a 60 billion yen eurobond due april 22, 1992 paying 4-1/4 pct and priced at 101-5/8 pct, lead manager nomura international ltd said. the non-callable issue will be available in denominations of one mln yen and will be listed in london. the fees comprise 1-1/4 pct selling concession with 5/8 pct management and underwriting combined. reuter 
