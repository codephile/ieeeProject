north korea said it agreed to a south korean proposal for talks between the prime ministers of the two countries, and proposed a preparatory meeting on april 23. the state-run north korean central news agency, monitored here, said pyongyang signalled its agreement in a letter sent by north korean prime minister li gun-mo to his southern counterpart lho shin-yong. lho proposed that the two prime ministers meet for talks in a letter on march 17. reuter 
