fpl group capital inc, a unit of fpl group inc, is raising 150 mln dlrs through an offering of debentures due 2017 yielding 8.951 pct, said lead manager salomon brothers inc. the debentures have an 8-7/8 pct coupon and were priced at 99.20 to yield 135 basis points over the off-the-run 9-1/4 pct treasury bonds of 2016. non-refundable for five years, the issue is rated a-2 by moody's and a-plus by standard and poor's. goldman sachs co-managed the deal. reuter 
