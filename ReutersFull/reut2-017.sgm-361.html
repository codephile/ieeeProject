the american stock exchange said an investment trust which issues units in exchange for eastman kodak co shares began trading today on a "when issued" basis. amex said the trust, known as the americus trust for kodak shares, trades under the ticker symbol kduwi and will accept a maximum of 7,500,000 shares. the trust included two categories which traded separately from the units, known as prime and score components, which respectively allowed investors to receive current income or capital appreciation potential, it said. reuter 
