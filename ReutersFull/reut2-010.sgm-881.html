grain traders and analysts expect lower wheat and soybean exports and higher corn exports than a year ago in the usda's export inspection report today. corn export guesses ranged from 27.0 mln to 32.0 mln bushels, compared with the 27.6 mln inspected last week and 20.5 mln a year ago. soybean export guesses ranged from 14.0 mln to 16.0 mln, up from the 13.4 mln inspected last week but below the 25.5 mln reported a year ago. wheat estimates ranged from 11.0 mln to 14.0 mln bushels, compared with 12.0 mln reported last week and 18.3 mln a year ago. reuter 
