clini-therm corp said a dallas court granted a temporary restraining order prohibiting a former officer and director from using or disclosing priveleged information obtained during employment at the company. the 95th judicial district court of dallas county set a hearing on april 8 to consider a preliminary injunction, the company said. the court ordered the former officer, victor vaguine, to appear at the hearing and show cause why he should not be enjoined from working for bsd medical corp, the company said. reuter 
