the singapore government has taken over 82 mln dlrs worth of goodwood park hotel ltd shares and loan stock certificates belonging to the company's chairman, khoo teck puat, banking sources said. khoo is a major shareholder of the (nbb), which closed in november after the brunei authorities alleged 90 pct of its loans of 1.3 billion brunei dlrs had been extended to khoo-related firms without documentation or guarantee. the goodwood securities are now held by the commercial affairs investigation department, the banking sources said. the department declined to comment. the government move is aimed at protecting goodwood's minority shareholders and securing goodwood deposits at the nbb, the banking sources said. goodwood had a total of 87.3 mln dlrs in fixed deposits, bank balances and accrued interest due from nbb as of last november, according to goodwood's annual report. reuter 
