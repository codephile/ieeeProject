unisys corp said shipments will begin in july 1987 of its new security software package to prevent unauthorized access to its a series mainframe computers used by commercial customers and for government installations. the infoguard software security module extends the security facilities already available on a series computers, it said. infoguard may be licensed through a five year extended term purchase or on a monthly license basis. the five year etp ranges from 8,450 dlrs on an a 2 mainframe to 34,300 dlrs on an a 15 mainframe, it said. reuter 
