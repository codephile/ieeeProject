the u.s. agriculture department said it has accepted a bid for an export bonus to cover the sale of 50,000 tonnes of hard red winter wheat to sri lanka. a bonus of 37.44 dlrs per tonne was awarded to continental grain co on the shipment scheduled for april 8-16, melvin sims, usda general sales manager said. an additional 10,000 tonnes of wheat are still available to sri lanka under the export enhancement program, sims said. reuter 
