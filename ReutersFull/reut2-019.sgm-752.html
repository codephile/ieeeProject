moody's investors service inc said it may downgrade 1.3 billion dlrs of debt of public service company of new hampshire. under review are b-1 first mortgage bonds, b-2 refunding mortgage bonds, b-3 third mortgage and secured pollution control revenue bonds, caa debentures and preferred stock. moody's cited an uncertain ability to meet fixed-income obligations due to the nuclear regulatory commission's refusal to grant a low-power operating license because of inadequate evacuation plans. the firm depends on credit markets to fund obligations, due to a negative internal cash flow, it said. reuter 
