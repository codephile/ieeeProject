hawaiian airlines inc said it will continue to expand services to pacific islands this year. the company said it will increase its south pacific services to daily service, from the current four flights per week, and beginning may 21 it will fly twice a week to the western pacific to guam. hawaiian airlines also said it has applied with the department of transportation for an emergency exemption to provide service to tahiti, with connecting services to american samoa, new zealand and cooke islands. reuter 
