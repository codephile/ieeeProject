columbia gas system inc said it has formed columbia erie pipeline corp as a subsidiary to participate with coastal corp's anr pipeline co in construction and operation of the erie pipeline system. as previously announced, columbia gas and anr pipeline signed a letter of intent to form a partnership to construct and operate the erie system, which will run from anr's facilities in defiance county, ohio, to clinton, county, pa. columbia said specific terms of participation are to be spelled out in a partnership agreement to be negotiated within the next 60 days. reuter 
