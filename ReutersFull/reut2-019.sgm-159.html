a target date of mid-september has been set for the signing of a package rescheduling nigeria's medium term public sector debt and outstanding letter of credit claims, nigeria and barclays bank plc said in a joint statement. the statement was issued during the last of a two day meeting here between the two sides. barclays is a co-chairman of the commercial bank steering committee. the statement said, "further progress has been made in the 1986/87 rescheduling of medium term public sector debt and outstanding letter of credit claims." reuter 
