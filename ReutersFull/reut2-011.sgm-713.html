avon products inc, the diversifed conglomerate that had a strong turn around in 1986, said it expects sales and earnings to climb higher this year. in its annual report, the company also said it expects to maintain its current annual two dlr dividend on the basis of continued upward earnings. in 1986, avon's operational earnings rose 24 pct to 158.7 mln dlrs from 128.2 mln dlrs a year earlier, and sales rose 17 pct to 2.88 billion dlrs. it said the 2.23 dlrs a share earned last year was the highest in five years, but still well below the company's all-time high of 4.06 dlrs a share in 1979. reuter 
