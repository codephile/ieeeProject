american express overseas credit corp is issuing a 50 mln new zealand dlr bond due april 27, 1990 carrying a coupon of 18 pct and priced at 101-1/4, said hambros bank ltd as lead manager. the issue is guaranteed by american express co. payment is set for april 27, 1987 and the securities will be listed on the luxembourg stock exchange. there is a one pct selling concession and a 1/2 pct combined underwriting and management fee. reuter 
