ireland is raising 300 mln marks through a 10-year eurobond with a 6-1/2 pct coupon priced at 100-1/2, lead manager commerzbank ag said. the bond, with a yield at issue of 6.43 pct, will pay annual interest on april 29. listing is in frankfurt, the bank said. investors will pay for the bond on april 29, and the bond matures on the same day in 1997. the bond will be issued in denominations of 1,000 and 10,000 marks. fees total 2-1/2 pct, with 1-1/2 points for selling and 1/2 each for management and underwriting. reuter 
