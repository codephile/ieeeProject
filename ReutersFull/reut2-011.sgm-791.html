the is planning a 100 mln australian dlr note issuance facility, lead manager westpac finance asia ltd said. the three year facility is half underwritten and half non-underwritten. the underwriting margin is 10 basis points over australian dlr bank bill rate and the underwriting fee is 7.5 basis points. there is an issuance fee of 2.5 basis points and an utilisation fee of 7.5 basis points if the underwriters are required to pick up 51 pct or more of the notes issued. the notes, in denominations of 100,000 dlrs, will have maturities ranging from one to six months. reuter 
