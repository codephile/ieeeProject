bearings inc said it filed with the securities and exchange commission a registration statement covering a 40 mln dlr issue of convertible subordinated debentures due 2012. proceeds will be used to fund substantially fund the redemption on october 26 of the company's 8-1/2 pct convertible subordinated debentures of 2012. bearings will buy back the convertible debt at 105.95 pct of 1,000 dlr face amount, plus accrued interest. the company said it named merrill lynch capital markets as underwriter of the offering. reuter 
