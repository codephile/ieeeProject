dutch green coffee imports fell to 10,430 tonnes in january from 13,506 tonnes in january 1986, and exports fell to 366 tonnes from 615, the central bureau of statistics said. imports of colombian coffee were 2,169 tonnes (3,025 in january 1986), brazilian 483 (3,715), indonesian 455 (145), guatemalan 196 (126), cameroun 464 (560) and ivory coast 353 (839). reuter 
