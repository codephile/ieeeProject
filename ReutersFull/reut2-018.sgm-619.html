the arab monetary fund is to lend iraq 71.7 mln dlrs to help to finance its trade with other arab countries, the united arab emirates news agency wam said. the first repayment on the loan will fall due in 30 months with full repayment spread over four years. the loan carries 4.95 pct interest in the first year, rising to 5.85 pct in the fourth year. today's agreement brings total loans by the fund to iraq since 1983 to 390.6 mln dlrs. reuter 
