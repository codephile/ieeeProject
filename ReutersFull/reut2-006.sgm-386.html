credit lyonnais is issuing a 19 billion yen zero coupon eurobond due april 15, 1992 priced at 81.22 pct, lead manager nomura international ltd said. the bond is available in denominations of 10 mln yen and will be listed in luxembourg. credit lyonnais is joint lead. fees comprise 85 basis points selling concession and 70 basis points for management and underwriting. payment date is april 15. reuter 
