a chinese company has won a 3.5 mln dlr contract to supply pumping stations for the biggest irrigation project ever undertaken in cyprus, an official announcement said. the southern conveyor project, to be completed in two years, aims to conserve over 90 pct of the island's water resources by 1990 with a network of huge dams. it will raise water storage capacity from six mln cubic meters in 1961 to 290 mln cubic meters. reuter 
