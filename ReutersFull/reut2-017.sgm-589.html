novell inc said it introduced a series of new networking products that extend its netware local area network (lan) communications. one of the new products, an asynchronous bridge, connects multiple remote netware lans to a local to a local netware lan over telephone lines using high-speed modems, novell said. novell also said it has introduced a new asynchronous gateway which provides netware lans with access to resources on minicomputers from digital equipment corp , data general , hewlett-packard , prime computer inc and tandem computers . novell also said that its cxi inc unit has introduced a series of new international business machines corp gateway products including a 40-session coaxial gateway software, a variety of lan workstation gateway sofware and new software for pcox gateways. reuter 
