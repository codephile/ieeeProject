deposit guaranty national bank said it will add 8.1 mln dlrs to its loan loss reserve during the second quarter to cover potential foreign debt exposure in latin america. it said the action will reduce second quarter earnings of deposit guaranty corp, the bank's parent, by about 60 cts a share. it said the corporation will have a profitable quarter and also a strong earnings year in 1987. following the increase, deposit guaranty said its loan loss reserve will total about 37 mln dlrs, or 1.9 pct of total outstanding loans. reuter 
