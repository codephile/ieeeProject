integrated resources inc said it filed with the securities and exchange commission a registration statement covering 150 mln dlrs of senior notes. the notes are to be sold in three equal-sized issues of 50 mln dlrs. the maturities will be 1990, 1992 and 1994. proceeds will be used to repay short-term debt incurred primarily for working capital, integrated resources said. the company named drexel burnham lambert inc as sole manager of the offerings. reuter 
