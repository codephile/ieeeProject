the belgo-luxembourg economic union recorded a provisional trade deficit of 9.45 billion francs in january after a december surplus of 15.32 billion francs, revised upwards from a provisional surplus of 11.94 billion, the national statistics office said. in january 1986, the union recorded a deficit of 23.31 billion francs. january imports fell to a provisional 228.86 billion francs from 240.24 billion in december and 281.65 billion a year earlier, but exports were also lower, at 219.41 billion francs against 255.56 billion and 258.34 billion respectively. reuter 
