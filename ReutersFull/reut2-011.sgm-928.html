first national bank in mount clemens, mich., said it is seeking final regulatory approval to form first national bank corp, a bank holding company, following shareholder approval tuesday. it said formation of first national bank corp will result in a one-for-one stock exchange for shareholders of first national bank in mount clemens. first national bank corp is the parent company of first national bank in mount clemens and bankers life insurance co, a newly created insurance subsidiary. at the shareholders' meeting, the bank also reported 1986 first national bank earnings of 2,008,000 dlrs, or 2.22 dlrs a share, up 11 pct from 1985's earnings of 1,811,000 dlrs, or 2.00 dlrs a share. bank assets climbed to 276 mln dlrs from 240 mln dlrs in 1985, it said. reuter 
