the municipality of metropolitan toronto is issuing a 75 mln canadian dlr eurobond due may 7, 1997 paying 8-3/4 pct and priced at 101-1/8 pct, lead manager wood gundy inc said. the bond will be listed in luxembourg and is available in denominations of 1,000 dlrs. fees comprise 1-1/4 pct selling concession and 3/8 pct each for management and underwriting. pay date is may 7. reuter 
