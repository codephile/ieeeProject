moody's investors service inc said it may upgrade the ratings on 1.45 billion dlrs of debt of sci holdings inc. the agency cited sci's announcement that it agreed to sell for 1.3 billion dlrs six television stations and related properties to a joint venture formed by sci and gillett holdings inc. while financing details are not available, the agency said sci's debt should decline as a result of the sale. bci currently carries ba-3 zero coupon senior notes and b-2 senior subordinated debentures. the bci unit storer communications inc has b-2 subordinated debentures. reuter 
