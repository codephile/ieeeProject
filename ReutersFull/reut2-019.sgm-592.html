french financial group metrologie international said it is issuing 73.8 mln francs worth of stock option bonds with banque paribas as lead manager. the eight-year issue at par will carry interest of six pct payable on august 3. existing shareholders will have priority subscription to the 1,000 franc nominal bonds on the basis of one bond for 14 shares held. each bond will be accompanied by two warrants giving the right to subscribe to one 40 franc nominal share at a price of 580 francs. reuter 
