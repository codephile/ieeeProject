showa aluminium corp is issuing a 100 mln dlr equity warrant eurobond due april 23, 1992 paying an indicated coupon of 2-1/8 pct and priced at par, lead manager nomura international ltd said. final terms will be fixed on april 15. the selling concession is 1-1/2 pct while management and underwriting combined pays 3/4 pct. the non-callable bond is available in denominations of 5,000 dlrs and will be listed in luxembourg. the payment date is april 23. reuter 
