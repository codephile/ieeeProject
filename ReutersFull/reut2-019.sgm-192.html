texaco inc's texaco canada said it will raise postings for its edmonton/swann hills crude by 24 canadian cts a barrel, effective june 20. the company said the new posting for edmonton/swann hills will be 25.60 dlrs a barrel. the price hike follows a round of crude oil price increases started late june 17 by sun co. the other major canadian crude suppliers raised prices june 18. reuter 
