american ecology corp said its national ecology inc unit has executed contracts valued at 142 mln dlrs with the babcock and wilcox co. under the contracts, american ecology will design, supply, start-up and support 20-year operations and maintenance of the refuse derived fuel receiving and processing part of the palm beach county, florida solid waste authority resource recovery facility. construction of the facility has begun. operations are scheduled to start by 1990, the company said. reuter 
