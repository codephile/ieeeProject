the national association of securities dealers inc. denied kirk knapp's application to become a registered member of the firm. nasd's board of governors said it denied the application of knapp to become registered with k.a. knapp and co, located in grand rapids, mich. the board said it based its decision on two statutory disqualifications. in the first case, the board said it found knapp failed to maintain rquired net capital, filed inaccurate focus part 1 reports and inaccurately calculated the amount required to be on deposit in the special reserve account. the second case involved knapp being enjoined from violations of sections of the securities exchange act, the nasd said. reuter 
