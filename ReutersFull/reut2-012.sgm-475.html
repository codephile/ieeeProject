und frankfurter bank (bhf) is raising 30 mln australian dollars through a five-year bullet eurobond with a 14-1/4 pct coupon and priced at 101-1/4, sole manager bhf said. the bond, for bhf finance jersey ltd, pays annual interest on april 28, and investors pay for the bond on the same day. it will mature on that day in 1992. the bond is guaranteed by the parent. listing is in luxembourg. denominations are 1,000 and 10,000 dlrs. yield at issue is 13.89 pct. no fees were stated. reuter 
