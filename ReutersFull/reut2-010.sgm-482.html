advanced tobacco products inc said it signed a letter of intent to license a smoking deterrent product to a unit of pharmacia ab of sweden. under terms of the agreement, the company said pharmacia's ab leo unit will pay an undisclosed sum, including non-refundable royalties, for exclusive rights to evaluate advanced tobacco's nicotine technology through july. the company said the unit will also have an option to buy exclusive worldwide licensing rights for the technology for additional cash and royalties. reuter 
