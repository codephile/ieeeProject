thailand's sugarcane output will fall to about 23.55 mln tonnes in the 1986/87 (november-october) season from 24.09 mln in 1985/86, the agriculture ministry said. it said a january survey put the total area under sugarcane at 545,528 hectares, down from 556,860 hectares the previous year. the national average yield is expected to fall to 43.17 tonnes/hectare from 43.22 tonnes in 1985/86, it said. reuter 
