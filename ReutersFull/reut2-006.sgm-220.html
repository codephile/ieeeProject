honduras has been authorized to buy about 75,000 tonnes of u.s. wheat, about 15,000 tonnes of u.s. corn, and about 6,000 tonnes of u.s. tallow under an existing pl 480 agreement, the u.s. agriculture department said. the department said it may buy the wheat, valued at 8.5 mln dlrs, the corn, valued at 1.5 mln, and the tallow, valued at 2.0 mln dlrs, between march 24 and august 31, 1987, and ship it from u.s. ports and/or canadian transshipment points by this september 30. the purchase authorizations cover the entire quantity provided under the agreement, signed march 11. reuter 
