asea group ab said it has been awarded a 110 mln dlr contract from the swedish state power board and the finnish utility . the company said the contract is for a 500 megawatt 400 kv fenno skan high voltage dc transmission to be built between sweden and finland. the company also said three other asea companies; asea transmission, ludvika sweden and strondberg vaasa in finland, received a related order for converter equipment. reuter 
