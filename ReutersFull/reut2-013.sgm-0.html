chrysler corp said car sales for the march 21-31 period rose 20.2 pct to 40,454 from 33,669 a year earlier. for the month of march, it said auto sales increased 6.3 pct to 96,649 from 90,945. chrysler said u.s. truck sales in late march jumped 65.2 pct to 28,698 from 17,372 a year ago. for the entire month, truck sales advanced 28.9 pct to 63,283 from 49,102, it said. the company said it was still tabulating year-to-date figures. reuter 
