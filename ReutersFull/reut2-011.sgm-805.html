caisse nationale des telecommunications is issuing a 20 billion yen eurobond due may 29, 1992 paying 4-3/8 pct and priced at 101-1/2 pct, lead manager ibj international ltd said. the non-callable bond is available in denominations of one mln yen and is guaranteed by france. the selling concession is 1-1/4 pct while management and underwriting combined pays 5/8 pct. the payment date is may 29 and the issue will be listed in luxembourg. reuter 
