french retail prices rose a confirmed 0.2 pct in february, in line with provisional figures released two weeks ago showing a rise of between 0.1 and 0.2 pct, the national statistics institute said. the rise compared with a 0.9 pct rise in january. year-on-year retail price inflation was confirmed at 3.4 pct for february compared with a three pct rise year-on-year in january. reuter 
