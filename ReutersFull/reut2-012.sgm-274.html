the u.s. commodity credit corporation (ccc) sold 6,034 short tons of 1986-crop farmers stock peanuts for domestic crushing, the u.s. agriculture department said. the peanuts were from the southwest area and were sold at between 8.05 cts per lb (total kernel content), and 11.7225 cts per lb, the department said. the ccc will offering additional 1986-peanuts for sale at a later date, the department said. reuter 
