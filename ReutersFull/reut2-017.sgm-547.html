the u.s. regional carrier has signed an agreement with saab scania ab to buy 16 34-seater saab sf340 aircraft worth a total 650 mln crowns, the swedish group's largest single order yet, saab said. it said in a statement that metro airlines was also taking an option on a further 15 sf340 commuter planes. the aircraft are to be delivered between now and next april. "the order from metro airlines is a definite breakthrough for the saab sf340," said tomy hjort, the head of the sf340 programme at saab's air division. reuter 
