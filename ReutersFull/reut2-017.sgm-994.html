bell canada, a unit of bell canada enterprises inc , said it applied for an average eight pct cut in canadian long distance rates. the application to the canadian radio-television and telecommunications commission would also reduce the initial period for operated-assisted calls from three minutes to one minute and operator- and customer-dialed calls would be charged the same. the billing of operator-assisted calls would include a conection charge, however. the new rate structure is similar to schedules announced by bell january 1 for calls within ontario, quebec and parts of the northwest territories, it said. reuter 
