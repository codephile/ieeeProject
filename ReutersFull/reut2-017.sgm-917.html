hidroelectrica iberica iberduero s.a. (iberduero) of bilbao, spain is issuing a 150 mln swiss franc eight year bond with a five pct coupon and 100-1/4 issue price, lead manager union bank of switzerland said. the bond is on sale until june 26 with payment due on july 9. it can be called at 101-1/2 in 1991 and for tax reasons only at 102 in 1988. reuter 
