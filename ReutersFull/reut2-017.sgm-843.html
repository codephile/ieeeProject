president amin gemayel named sunni moslem education minister selim hoss as acting prime minister following the assassination of premier rashid karami, a presidential palace statement said. "after consultations by president amin gemayel with political and religious leaders the president decided to name selim hoss as acting prime minister," said the statement broadcast on local radio stations. moslem leaders proposed hoss, a former premier, as acting prime minister after karami died of injuries from a bomb that exploded aboard his helicopter. reuter 
