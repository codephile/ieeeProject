carolco pictures inc said it signed a letter of intent to exchange 2.2 mln of its shares for all orbis communications inc stock within the next 60 days. it said the carolco shares used in the exchange are valued at 7.00 dlrs a share or a total value of 15.4 mln dlrs and will be adjusted based on the price of carolco shares on the closing date. it said orbis management will remain in place for at least three years. reuter 
