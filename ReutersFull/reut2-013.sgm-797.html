caisse nationale des autoroutes is issuing a 120 mln ecu, 7-3/8 pct bond due may 15, 1995 at 101-3/4 pct, lead manager credit lyonnais (cl) said. the non-callable issue of senior debt, guaranteed by france, has fees of 1-1/4 pct for selling and 5/8 pct for management and underwriting including a 1/8 pct praecipuum. "a management group is being put together and we are selling well inside the fees," a cl spokesman said. payment date is may 15, denominations are of 1,000 and 10,000 ecus and listing is in luxembourg. co-leads are shearson lehman and nippon european bank sa. reuter 
