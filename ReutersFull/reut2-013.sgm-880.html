brierley investments overseas nv is issuing a 100 mln dlr eurobond due april 30, 1992 paying 8-1/4 pct and priced at par, lead manager banque paribas capital markets said. the non-callable bond is available in denominations of 5,000 dlrs and will be listed in luxembourg. the selling concession is 1-1/4 pct while management and underwriting combined pays 5/8 pct. the issue is guaranteed by brierley investments ltd. payment date is april 30. reuter 
