piedmont airlines said its total cargo load in april increased 39.5 pct compared to april 1986. piedmont said in april 1987 it carried a total of 4,949,369 ton miles of cargo compared to 3,546,543 ton miles last april. in addition, the airline said in the first four months of 1987, total cargo load increased 30 pct to 18.2 mln ton miles from 14.0 mln ton miles over the same period last year. reuter 
