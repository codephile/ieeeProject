ford motor co said it made 189,610 u.s. cars in march, up from 150,520 in the same period last year. ford said it made 143,949 trucks during the month, compared to 116,727 in the 1986 period. year-to-date, ford said it produced 534,209 u.s. cars, up from 446,505 last year-to-date. truck production through the end of march was 403,836 vehicles, up from 362,986 vehicles, ford said. reuter 
