joy technologies inc is raising 185 mln dlrs through an offering of senior subordinated debentures due 1999 yielding 13.85 pct, said lead manager kidder, peabody and co inc. the notes have a 13-3/4 pct coupon and were priced at 99.396, kidder said. the issue is non-callable for five years. moody's rates the debt b-1, compared to a b-minus grade by standard and poor's. donaldson, lufkin and jenrette co-managed the deal. reuter 
