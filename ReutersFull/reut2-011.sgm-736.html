taco villa inc said it is holding talks with republicbank corp to restructure its loan payments and waive some financial convenants on its debt. it also said it may sell certain assets to repay the bank borrowings, and is holding talks with interested parties. it expects to write down some previously acquired assets, but the company did not say by how much. at the end of 1986 it had 11 mln dlrs in bank debt outstanding with a 1.7 mln dlr payment due may one. it is also seeking to extend 1.2 mln dlrs of mortgage payments due this year, and is in talks to extend other debt payments. the company said it previously reported special charges of nine mln dlrs in the fourth quarter from losses on restaurant closings, lease termination costs, contract settlements and severance arrangements. since it has not yet decided how to treat the additional writedowns and debt, it has delayed its 1986 financial statement. it said it expects to file an amendment to its form 10-k but did not say when the amended filing would be made. the company said it hoped to resolve its debt problems in the near future. reuter 
