japan's livestock industry promotion corp (lipc) said that on april 28 it will import 7,515 tonnes of beef for the first half of the 1987 fiscal year started on april 1 under the simultaneous buy and sell tender system, against 6,813 a year ago. the system calls on the agency to buy beef from trading houses and simultaneously sell it to users in one tender. the tender quota in april-september is 10 pct of the agency's beef import share. japan's beef import quota in 1987/88 was set at 93,000 tonnes against 85,000 for the same period in 1986/87. reuter 
