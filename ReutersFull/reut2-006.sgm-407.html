land securities plc is issuing a 100 mln stg eurobond due april 29, 2007 with a 9-1/2 pct coupon and priced at 95-3/4, lead manager j. henry schroder wagg and co ltd said. the non-callable bonds will be listed in london and will be issued in denominations of 1,000 and 10,000 stg. fees are 1-1/2 pct for selling and one pct for management and underwriting combined with a 3/16 pct praecipuum. the pay date is april 29. reuter 
