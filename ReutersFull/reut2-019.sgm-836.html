lomas and nettleton financial corp said its shareholders approved at a special meeting the company's acquisition of equitable life leasing corp for 263.3 mln dlrs. equitable life leasing corp is a subsidiary of equitable investment corp, which is owned by the equitable life assurance co of u.s. lomas said the acquistion price will consist of one mln in cash, 1.5 mln shares of the company's common stock, 71,000 shares of a new series c preferred stock, and 8.944 mln dlrs principal amount of nine pct senior notes due 1994. the company added that the acquisition will close june 30. reuter 
