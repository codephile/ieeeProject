merrill lynch and co inc is offering in the domestic debt market 75 mln dlrs of new zealand dollar-denominated notes due 1988, said sole manager merrill lynch capital markets. the notes were assigned a 20 pct coupon and priced at par to yield 150 basis points below comparable new zealand rates. non-callable for life, the issue is rated a-1 by moody's and aa by standard and poor's corp. the notes have an annual interest coupon instead of paying interest semi-annually, merrill said. reuter 
