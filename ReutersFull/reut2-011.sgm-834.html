the u.s. dollar convertible eurobond for keppel corp has been increased to 75 mln dlrs from the initial 60 mln, bookrunner morgan grenfell and co ltd said. the coupon was set at four pct compared with an indication of four to 4-1/4 pct. the conversion price was set at 3.12 singapore dlrs per share, a premium of 5.05 pct. the foreign exchange rate was set at 2.1381 singapore dlrs to the u.s. dollar. reuter 
