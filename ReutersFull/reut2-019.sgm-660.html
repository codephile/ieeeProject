the exercise price for the warrants attached to the 100 mln dlr eurobond for finland's nokia oy has been fixed, lead manager morgan guaranty ltd said. the five year, par-priced, deal was launched last thursday and pays five pct. each 5,000 dlr bond has 134 warrants attached which are each exercisable into one nokia free preferred share at a price of 169.4 finnish markka. this compares with today's closing price of 178 markka. reuter 
