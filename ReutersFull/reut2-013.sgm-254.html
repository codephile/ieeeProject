china's foreign exchange reserves totalled 10.514 billion dlrs at end-1986, up from 10.37 billion at end-september but down from 11.9 billion at end-1985, according to central bank figures published by the new china news agency. the agency said 2.072 billion dlrs of the reserves was held by the state treasury, down from 2.26 billion at end-september, while 8.442 billion was held by the bank of china, up from 8.11 billion. china's outstanding foreign debts rose to 7.572 billion at end-1986 from 5.067 billion at end-1985. gold reserves stood at 12.67 mln ounces, unchanged over the year. reuter 
