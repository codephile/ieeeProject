manhattan federal court judge shirley wohl kram said she would rule monday on whether to grant a request to block a 78 dlr per share merger agreement between burlington industries inc and morgan stanely group inc . the request to bar the merger was made by samjens acquisition corp, a partnership formed by financier asher b. edelman and dominion textiles inc. samjens has made a hostile 77 dlr per share takeover bid for burlington. reuter 
