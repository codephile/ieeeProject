great western financial corp said it agreed to sell its john alden life insurance co and its affiliated operations for 280 mln dlrs to a newly-formed company onwed by the john alden management group. general electric credit corp delivered a commitment letter arranged by the gecc capital markets group inc for the financing. great western said the pre-tax gain on the sale will be approximately 65 mln dlrs, and after tax gain will be approximately 15 mln. reuter 
