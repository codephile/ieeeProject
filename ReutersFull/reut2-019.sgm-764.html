mitsubishi motor sales of america inc said it is raising car prices by an average 1.8 pct, or 227 dlrs and truck prices by 1.6 pct, or 150 dlrs, because of the continued strength of the japanese yen against the u.s. dollar. the company also said the price for its montero sport/utility vehicle will be increased by 1.5 pct, or 150 dlr. the changes are effective immediately, mitsubishi also said. reuter 
