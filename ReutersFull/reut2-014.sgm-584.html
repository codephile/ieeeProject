terrace bank of florida said it is selling 500,000 shares of stock at 10.50 dlrs per share in its initial offering. the organizers of the bank plan to purchase 81,000 of those shares, which will have a par value of five dlrs. individual investors may purchase as few as 100 shares of the stock, the bank said. the offering is slated to end by may 15, but could be extended, it said. reuter 
