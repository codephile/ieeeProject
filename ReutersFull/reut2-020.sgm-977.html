treasury balances at the federal reserve rose on october 16 to 12.813 billion dlrs from 9.985 billion dlrs on the previous business day, the treasury said in its latest budget statement. balances in tax and loan note accounts rose to 28.398 billion dlrs from 28.117 billion dlrs on the same respective days. the treasury's operating cash balance totaled 41.211 billion dlrs on october 16 compared with 38.102 billion dlrs on october 15. reuter 
