hudson's bay co said it agreed to sell its entire 54.5 pct interest in canadian roxy petroleum ltd, a total of about 7.5 mln shares, to westcoast transmission co ltd for 11 dlrs a share. hudson's bay said proceeds of about 82 mln dlrs will be used to reduce corporate debt. it said the sale was part of a program of concentrating on its core business of department stores and real estate. the sale is subject to regulatory approvals. reuter 
