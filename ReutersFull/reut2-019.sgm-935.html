moody's investors service inc said it affirmed 1.2 billion dlrs of debt of the bank of new york co inc and units. affirmed were the parent's a-1 senior notes and preferred stock, a-2 subordinated capital notes and prime-1 commercial paper. also affirmed were the bank of new york's aa-2 long-term deposits and prime-1 short-term obligations. moody's cited the firm's announced 135 mln dlr addition to the loan loss reserve as a reflection of exposure to borrowers in less-developed countries. the addition adjusts the firm's financial statements to reflect economic reality, it said. reuter 
