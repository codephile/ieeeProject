atari corp said it has canceled a planned 75 mln dlr domestic offering of convertible subordinated debentures in favor of an overseas offering. yesterday, in london, lead underwriter painewebber group inc announced a planned offering of 75 mln dlrs of atari convertible debentures due 2002 with an indicated coupon of five to 5-1/4 pct. atari said the debentures will not be available for u.s. resal until at least 90 days after completion of the offering abroad. reuter 
