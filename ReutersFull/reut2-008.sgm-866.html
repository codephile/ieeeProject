leeds permanent building society is issuing a 50 mln stg offering of notes due april 23, 1992 carrying a coupon of 9-1/4 pct and priced at 100-7/8, said barings securities co as lead manager. bankers trust international is co-lead manager. the issue is partly paid, with only 20 pct of the payment due on april 23 and the remainder due october 23. the issue is priced to yield 50 basis points over comparable maturity gilts. there is a 1-1/4 pct selling concession and a 5/8 pct management and underwriting fee. reuter 
