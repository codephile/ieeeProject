cbs inc is issuing 400 mln dlrs of convertible debt due april 7, 2002, with a coupon of five pct and priced at par, said morgan stanley ltd as lead manager. the securities are convertible into shares of cbs at a price of 200 dlrs per share, which represents a premium of 26.2 pct over the company's closing stock price yesterday of 158.50 dlrs on the new york stock exchange. the issue is callable in the first three years only if the stock prices rises abouve 130 pct of the conversion level. there is a 1-1/2 pct selling concession and a one pct combined management and underwriting fee. reuter 
