china has switched purchases of u.s. wheat totaling 60,000 tonnes from the 1986/87 season, which began june 1, to 1987/88 season delivery, the u.s. agriculture department said. the department said outstanding wheat sales to china for the current season amount to 90,000 tonnes and sales for delivery in the 1987/88 season amount to 910,000 tonnes. total corn commitments for the 1986/87 season total 1,015,800 tonnes, the department said. reuter 
