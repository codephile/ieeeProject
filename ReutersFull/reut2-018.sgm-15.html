q-med inc said preliminary results show its second quarter, ended may 31, revenues exceeded 4.2 mln dlrs. a year earlier revenues were nearly 1.4 mln dlrs. the company also said it had appointed coopers and lybrand as its auditors. chairman michael cox declined any comment on this appointment. the company said its preliminary second quarter revenues are consistent with management's expectations and represent a greater than 40 pct increase in sales over those reported for the first quarter. earnings should show a similar growth pattern, the company added. reuter 
