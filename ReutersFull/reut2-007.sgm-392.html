yamakawa manufacturing corp of america said it began construction of a 17 mln dlrs manufacturing plant in portland, tenn. the plant will produce metal parts for automobile manufacturers, including nissan motor manufacturing corp usa. long-term plans call for the addition of stamping capabilities and pressed plastic parts to its product line. assembly operations are expected to begin in october 1987. the plant is being built to employ 150 people. yamakawa is a wholly-owned subsidiary of yamakawa industrial co ltd of japan. reuter 
