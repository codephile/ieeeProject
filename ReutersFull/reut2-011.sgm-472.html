california energy co inc said drexel burnham lambert inc is highly confident it can provide the company with 160 mln dlrs of private financing, subject to certain conditions. california energy said that as part of the financing, it received a letter of commitment for 133 mln dlrs of project funding from credit suisse. the company said proceeds of the financing will be used by its china lake joint venture for the purchase of equipment and construction of an 80-megawatt power plant at a company-developed geothermal site at china lake, calif. reuter 
