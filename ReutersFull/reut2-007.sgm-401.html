transamerica corp said its board authorized the repurchase of up to 2.2 mln shares of its outstanding stock in addition to the 1.5 mln shares previously authorized. the company has 72.7 mln shares outstanding. it said the reacquired 2.2 mln shares will be used to meet excercise requirements for its stock options plans, while the previously approved 1.5 mln shares may be used for employee benefit plan or other corporate purposes. reuter 
