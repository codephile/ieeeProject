micron products inc said it has agreed to acquire numedco inc from for 500,000 micron common shares, 2,400 shares of 1,000 dlrs par value preferred and an undisclosed amount of cash. the company said the acquisition has been approved by micron and myraid directors and is subject to approval by shareholders of both companies. micron now has about 1.9 mln common outstanding. numedco could increase micron's sales by 800 pct and its earnings by 100 pct, it said. in the nine months ended march 31, it earned 269,000 dlrs on sales of 2.7 mln dlrs. reuter 
