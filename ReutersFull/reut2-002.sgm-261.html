amro bank nv announced a 150 mln guilder seven-year dual-currency bond issue with a 6.5 pct coupon on behalf of bp capital bv, guaranteed by british petroleum co plc subsidiary bp maatschappij nederland bv. the par-priced public issue will be redeemed in full at 487.80 dlrs per 1,000 guilder bond on april 15, 1994. amro said the bonds, the second dual-currency issue in the dutch market in two weeks, is aimed at investors who expect the dollar to rise in the medium term. interest payments during the life of the loan are, however, not exposed to exchange rate fluctuations. early redemption will not be allowed. subscriptions on the loan close at 1400 gmt on march 13. payment date is april 15. the issue is underwritten by a management group led by amro, algemene bank nederland nv and pierson, heldring and pierson nv. reuter 
