aristech chemical corp, the former chemicals subsidiary of usx corp , said it expects its earnings for 1987 to exceed analysts estimates of up to 2.50 dlrs a share. aristech earlier reported net income of 18.7 mln dlrs or 72 cts a share compared with 14.4 mln dlrs or 56 cts a share in the year-earlier period on a pro forma basis. last year, aristech earned 43.9 mln dlrs on a pro forma basis, as if the company were a stand-alone entity effective january one, 1986. reuter 
