mca inc said noted film maker steven spielberg will serve as creative consultant for universal studios tour florida. the tour is a joint venture between mca and cineplex odeon corp that is scheduled to open in 1989. mca is also planning a major expansion of its universal studio tours. the expansion includes four live-action shows based films and tv series, including spielberg's "e.t.". mca's actions are seen as spurred by the disney co's chairman michal eisner's intention to build a new attraction in the los angeles area, possibly a studio tour. reuter 
