martin processing inc said its board approved an agreement to be acquired by courtaulds plc for 20 dlrs a share. the company said the transaction is valued at more than 99 mln dlrs, based on its 4,963,620 shares of stock outstanding. it said courtaulds has privately agreed to purchase a majority of martin's common from trusts established by julius hermes for 20 dlrs a share. it said courtaulds intends to conduct a tender offer for all the martin stock it does not own, beginning as soon as necessary documents are prepared. reuter 
