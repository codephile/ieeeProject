great western bank, a unit of great western financial corp, is offering 200 mln dlrs of notes due 1997 yielding 9.60 pct, said lead manager merrill lynch capital markets. the notes have a 9-1/2 pct coupon and were priced 99.365 to yield 130 basis points more than comparable treasury securities. non-callable to maturity, the issue is rated a-2 by moody's and a by standard and poor's. the gross spread is 6.50 dlrs, the selling concession is four dlrs and the reallowance is 2.50 dlrs. shearson lehman co-managed the deal. reuter 
