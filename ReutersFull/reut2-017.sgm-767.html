the world bank said it will provide 37.1 mln dlrs to tanzania and rwanda through the international development association (ida), the bank's concessionary lending affiliate. tanzania will recieve a 23 mln dlr ida loan to help rehabilitate its telecommunications network, the bank said. the 14.1 mln dlr ida loan to rwanda will help finance tropical forest conservation projects and a reforestation program, the bank said. the ida credit is for 50 years, including 10 years of grace, and carries no interest, the bank said. reuter 
