tokyo optical co ltd is issuing a 40 mln dlr equity warrant eurobond due april 27, 1992 paying an indicated coupon of 2-1/4 pct and priced at par, lead manager daiwa europe ltd said. the issue is guaranteed by mitsui bank ltd and final terms will be set on april 9. the issue is available in denominations of 5,000 dlrs and will be listed in luxembourg. the selling concession is 1-1/2 pct while management and underwriting combined pays 3/4 pct. the warrants are exercisable from may 19, 1987 until april 9, 1992. the payment date is april 27. reuter 
