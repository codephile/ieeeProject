u.s. subsidiary, siemens public switching systems inc said two existing plants have been selected to allow immediate start up of manufacturing on an interim basis of ewsd digital central office switching systems in the u.s. the company said the two facilities, one in cherry hill, n.j., the other in hauppauge, n.y., will be used while a rigorous review process for selecting a facility devoted only to ewsd manufacturing continues. siemens public is part of siemens communication systems inc, also headquartered in boca raton. reuter 
