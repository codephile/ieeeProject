turnover on the london international financial futures exchange (liffe) rose to 1.16 mln contracts in march, up from 517,746 a year earlier, figures issued by the international commodities clearing house (icch) show. turnover for the whole of the first quarter was 2.87 mln contracts against 1.52 mln in the same period last year. the biggest increases in march were seen for gilt options, long gilt futures and short sterling, the icch figures show. key figures given by the icch were as follows- march 87 march 86 gilt options 92,182 19,046 stg/dlr options 2,640 14,375 ftse 25,741 10,444 short sterling 147,863 72,118 20-year gilt 700,102 172,564 eurodollars 107,988 71,671 t-bonds 72,319 136,749 reuter 
