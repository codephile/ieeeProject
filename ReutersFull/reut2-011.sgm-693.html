honduras will tender april 2 for u.s. and non-u.s. flag vessels to import 19,369 tonnes of wheat in bulk, an agent for the country said. the agent said honduras is seeking vessels to deliver 7,369 tonnes during a period that includes laydays of april 15-30, and 12,000 tonnes with laydays of may 15-30. offers are due no later than 1200 hrs est, april 2, and will remain valid through the close of business the following day, the agent said. reuter 
