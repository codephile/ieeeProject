city of bergen, norway, is issuing an 80 mln canadian dlr eurobond, due april 13, 1994, priced at 101-1/2 with an 8-5/8 pct coupon, lead manager bank of tokyo international ltd said. the issue will be sold in denominations of 5,000 dlrs and listed in luxembourg. fees are 1-1/4 pct for selling and 5/8 pct for management and underwriting combined. payment date is april 13, 1987. there will be two co-lead managers. reuter 
