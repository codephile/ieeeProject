the china international trust and investment corp (citic) will issue a 30 billion yen, 10-year bond on april 23, daiwa securities said as lead manager. the samurai bond, to be signed on on april 14, will be the first since an asian development bank issue in february. the coupon is expected to be around five pct, daiwa said. the april japanese government 10-year bond coupon has been set at 4.7 pct against five pct on the march bond. reuter 
