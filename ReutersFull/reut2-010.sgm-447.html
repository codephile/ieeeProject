seven-up cos, a unit of philip morris cos inc, is raising 155.78 mln dlrs via an offering of senior subordinated notes due 1997 yielding 12-1/8 pct, said lead underwriter donaldson, lufkin and jenrette securities corp. the notes have a 7-1/2 pct coupon and were priced at 89.87. non-callable for three years, the debt is rated b-3 by moody's investors service inc and ccc-plus by standard and poor's corp. the issue was raised from an initial offering of 140 mln dlrs. reuter 
