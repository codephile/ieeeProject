ford motor credit co, a unit of ford motor co, is raising 150 mln dlrs through an offering of notes due 1990 yielding 8.275 pct, said sole manager bear, stearns and co. the notes have an 8-1/4 pct coupon and were priced at 99.931 to yield 62.5 basis points more than comparable treasury securities. non-callable to maturity, the issue is rated aa-2 by moody's and aa-minus by standard and poor's. the gross spread is 3.50 dlrs, the selling concession is two dlrs and the reallowance is 1.25 dlrs. reuter 
