treasury balances at the federal reserve fell on april 2 to 3.775 billion dlrs from 4.602 billion dlrs on the previous business day, the treasury said in its latest budget statement. balances in tax and loan note accounts fell to 6.493 billion dlrs from 6.495 billion dlrs on the same respective days. the treasury's operating cash balance totaled 10.268 billion dlrs on april 2 compared with 11.097 billion dlrs on april 1. reuter 
