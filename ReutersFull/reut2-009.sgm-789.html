guinness peat group plc has arranged to receive a 125 mln dlr five year cash advance facility, said barclays de zoete wedd ltd and guinness mahon and co ltd as joint arrangers. the two firms said the terms of the facility will be reviewed after the third year with a view to considering extension by a further two years. the facility will include a tender panel to bid for multi-currency cash advances at a maximum rate of 0.1875 pct over the london interbank offered rate. the facility will incorporate an underwriting fee of 0.10 pct in addition to a fee of 0.05 to 0.10 pct depending on the participation amount. in addition, barclays and guinness mahon have been named joint dealers for a complementary 100 mln dlr euro-commercial paper program. funds will be used to refinance the cost of the recent forstmann-leff associates acquisition as well as for general corporate purposes. reuter 
