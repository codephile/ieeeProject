benetton group spa said it reached agreement with textiles concern gft-gruppo finanziario tessile spa for a joint venture in the financial services sector. a benetton spokeswoman said details of the accord would be outlined at a news conference on thursday in milan. the italian clothing group has targeted financial services as a sector in which to expand its activities. in january, benetton acquired a large minority stake in the italian unit of the u.k. financial services group prudential corp plc [prul.l]. reuter 
