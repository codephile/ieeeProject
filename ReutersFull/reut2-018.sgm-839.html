conoco inc, a subsidiary of dupont denemours
, said it raised the contract price it will pay for all grades of crude oil 50 cts a barrel, effective yesterday. the increase brings conoco's posted price for west texas intermediate to 19.00 dlrs a barrel. the west texas sour grade, at 34 api, now stands at 18.10 dlrs a barrel. light louisiana was also raised 50 cts to 19.35 dlrs barrel. conoco last changed its crude postings on may 21. reuter 
