western digital corp said it plans to call for redemption on april 6 all of its approximately 47 mln dlrs principal amount of 6-3/4 pct convertible subordinated debentures due 2011. it said the debentures will be redeemable for 1,090.94 dlrs, including accrued interest, for each 1,000 dlrs principal amount. the debentures will be convertible into western digital common stock up until the redemption date at a conversion price of 17.50 dlrs per share, the company also said. reuter 
