burlington industries inc said it filed with the securities and exchange commission registration statements covering two offerings of debt securities. one issue will be 100 mln dlrs of sinking fund debentures due 2017. proceeds will be used for general corporate purposes, including the reduction of short-term borrowings, acquisitions, stock repurchases and capital expenditures. the other offering will be 75 mln dlrs of convertible subordinated debentures due 2012. proceeds will be used to redeem the company's outstanding 8-3/4 pct convertible subordinated debentures of 2008, burlington said. reuter 
