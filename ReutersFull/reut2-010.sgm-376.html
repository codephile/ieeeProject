japan's consumer price index (base 1985) was unchanged at 99.7 in february from a month earlier, the government's management and coodination agency said. the index showed a 0.4 pct drop in january. the february index was down one pct from a year earlier for the third consecutive year-on-year drop. in january, the index fell 1.1 pct from a year earlier, the first drop of over one pct since a 1.3 pct drop in september 1958. reuter 
