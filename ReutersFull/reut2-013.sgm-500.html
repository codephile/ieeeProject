malaysian crude palm oil futures closed off the day's highs on late speculative selling following talk that pakistan passed on its tender for another 6,000 tonnes of rbd palm oil for second half april shipment after buying the same amount yesterday, dealers said. a higher overnight chicago soyoil futures close had supported prices earlier, they said. june and july closed seven ringgit firmer at 746 and 742 ringgit per tonne respectively, while april and may were six and five ringgit higher at 747. turnover rose to 448 lots from 135 yesterday. reuter 
