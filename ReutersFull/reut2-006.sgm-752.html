hyponex corp is raising 150 mln dlrs through an offering of senior subordinated debentures due 1999 yielding 11.828 pct, said sole underwriter drexel burnham lambert inc. the debentures have an 11-3/4 pct coupon and were priced at 99.50, drexel said. the issue is non-callable for five years. there were no ratings by moody's investors service inc or standard and poor's corp at the time of the pricing, drexel said. hyponex said proceeds will be used mainly to finance acquisitions of assets and businesses. reuter 
