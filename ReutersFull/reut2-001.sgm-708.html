resource exploration inc said it has agreed to let drill 50 oil and natural gas wells on its clinton sandstone formation within its tuscarawas and harrison county, ohio area of operation. resource said it would receive a cash payment and an overriding royalty interest on oil and gas production from wells drilled on the property. resource said gas produced from the property will be transported through its existing pipeline. also, resource said it will provide service work to complete the wells and it will operate the wells after they are completed. reuter 
