skylark co ltd said it will issue a 20 billion yen nine-year unsecured convertible bond in the domestic capital market through public placement with nomura securities co ltd as lead manager. coupon and conversion price for par-priced bond maturing june 28, 1996, will be set at its board meeting to be held in mid-june and payment is due on july 1, the company said in a statement. skylark's share price fell 10 yen to 2,880 on the tokyo stock exchange today. reuter 
