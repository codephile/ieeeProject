lead underwriter painewebber group inc said an offering of two mln shares of the class a preferred stock of southern co's georgia power co subsidiary is underway at 25 dlrs per share. it said the underwriters won the issue at competitive sale today on a bid of 23.812 dlrs per share. the shares are not redeemable through refunding operations before june 1, 1992, when a sinking fund will start retiring the issue. reuter 
