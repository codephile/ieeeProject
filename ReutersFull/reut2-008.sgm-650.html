alcatel cit, the french subsidiary of which groups the worldwide telecommunications business of itt corp and france's state-owned cie generale d'electricite , has won a 15 mln french franc contract to supply transmission equipment to the swedish armed forces, the company said in a communique here. the order is for digital multiplexers. at the same time french computer services group said it has won a five mln franc contract to develop a data system for the swedish aeronautical research institute. reuter 
