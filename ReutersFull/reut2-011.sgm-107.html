the student loan marketing association announced an offering of australian 100 mln dlrs (approx. u.s. 70 mln) in two-year fixed rate notes priced at par and bearing interest at 14 pct per annum. sallie mae said semiannual interest payments and principal at maturity will be paid in australian dollars, adding it will enter into a separate currency and interest exchange agreement to convert its payment obligations to u.s. dollars. the notes, offered in u.s. capital markets by salomon brothers inc, are due april 14, 1989, and are for settlement april 14 with the first interest payment being made oct 14. reuter 
