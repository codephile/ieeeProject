mafina bv of voorburg, a subsidiary of petrofina sa , is issuing a minimum 200 mln franc seven year bond with warrants, paying two pct, lead manager credit suisse said. the issue is guaranteed by the belgian parent. the warrant exercise period is from may 1, 1987 until may 20, 1991. each 5,000 franc note carries nine warrannts, each of which entitles the holder to buy one share in the company at 10,800 belgian francs each. payment is due april 30. reuter 
