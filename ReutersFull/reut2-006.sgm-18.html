rabobank nederland b.a. said it is launching under its own management a 250 mln guilder eight-year bullet bond with a 6-3/4 pct coupon and priced at par. no early redemption is permitted on the bond which will be paid back in full on april 15, 1995. denominations are in 1,000 and five times 1,000 guilders. subscription closes march 23, payment is april 15. listing will be on the amsterdam stock exchange. reuter 
