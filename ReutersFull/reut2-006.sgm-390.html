a kidnapped saudi arabian diplomat has been freed in beirut after 64 days in captivity, witnesses said. they identified him as bakr damanhuri, responsible for saudi arabian students at beirut universities. damanhuri appeared at a news conference given by shi'ite moslem amal militia leader nabih berri, syria's military intelligence chief in lebanon brigadier ghazi kanaan and lebanese-born saudi arabian businessman rafiq hariri. reuter 
