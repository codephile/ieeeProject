henley group inc's m.w. kellogg co unit said it was selected by to design, engineer and build a ethylene plant at chinese petroleum's kaohsiung refinery in taiwan. terms of the contract were not disclosed, but kellogg said the total cost of the plant will be 300 mln dlrs. kellogg said engineering of the plant, which will have a capacity of 400,000 tonnes a year, is already underway and construction will begin in 1988. the plant will go into operation in 1990, kellogg said. reuter 
