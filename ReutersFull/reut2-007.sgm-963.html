yankee cos inc's eskey inc subsidiary said it reached an agreement in principle to sell its eskey's yale e. key inc subsidiary to a new concern formed by key's management and a private investor for about 15.5 mln dlrs. as part of sale, eskey said the buyers will assume the 14.5 mln dlrs of publicly held eskey 10-3/4 pct debentures due 2003. it said the debentures will continue to be converted into yankee preferred. the remainder of the price will be a one mln dlr note to eskey. yankee said the sale will result in a loss of 1.5 mln dlrs. reuter 
