infinite graphics inc expects earnings for the fourth quarter ending april 30 to exceed the 127,587 dlrs, or seven cts a share posted a year ago, chairman clifford f. stritch jr. told a meeting of financial analysts. stritch also said the company's annual revenues should exceed 6.0 mln dlrs, up from 4.1 mln dlrs in the previous fiscal year. he said the company, which markets advanced computer-aided engineering, design and manufacturing systems, plans to double its dealers to 50 during the coming fiscal year. reuter 
