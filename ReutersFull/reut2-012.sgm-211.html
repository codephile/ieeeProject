rio grande drilling co said its subsidiary, rio grande video, entered into a limited partnership agreement with a group of private investors to develop and operate five blockbuster video superstores in florida. blockbuster video superstores are franchises of blockbuster entertainment corp . rio grande video will act as general partner, owning 50 pct of the partnership, it said. the company must open 10 stores within the next two years to maintain exclusive license rights in the territory, it said. reuter 
