tunisia has bought 150,000 tonnes of french soft wheat for august to december shipment at 76.70 dlrs per tonne fob with coface export credit, trade sources said. this is the second french soft wheat export sale for the 1987/88 season which starts on july 1 and follows a brazilian purchase of 150,000 tonnes. france sold around 300,000 tonnes of soft wheat to tunisia during the 1986/87 season. reuter 
