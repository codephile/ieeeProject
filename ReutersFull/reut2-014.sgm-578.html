the u.s. treasury said it will sell 13.2 billion dlrs of three and six-month bills at its regular auction next week. the april 13 sale, to be evenly divided between the three and six month issues, will result in a paydown of 12.35 billion dlrs as maturing bills total 25.55 billion dlrs including the 9-day cash management bills issued april 7 in the amount of 11.01 billion dlrs. the bills will be issued april 16. reuter 
