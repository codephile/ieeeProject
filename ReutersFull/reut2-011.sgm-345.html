silicon graphics inc is raising 35 mln dlrs via an offering of convertible subordinated debentures due 2012 with a 5-3/4 pct coupon and par pricing, said lead manager morgan stanley and co inc. the debentures are convertible into the company's common stock at 30.35 dlrs per share, representing a premium of 25.15 pct over the stock price when terms on the debt were set. non-callable for two years, the issue is rated b-2 by moody's investors service inc and ccc-plus by standard and poor's corp. alex brown and sons inc co-managed the deal. reuter 
