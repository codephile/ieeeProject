simmons airlines inc said its board of directors authorized a doubling in the company's stock repurchase program to one mln shares from 500,000 shares. the buyback would represent about 20 pct of its issued shares, simmons said. the company said it bought 205,000 shares today, bringing its total repurchase since the program was authorized on july 29 to 534,500 shares. it said simmons now has 4,540,459 shares outstanding. its stock closed at 5-5/8 bid and 5-3/4 offered. shares will be bought privately and in the open market, it said. reuter 
