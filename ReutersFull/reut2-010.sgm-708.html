singapore welcomed the london metal exchange's (lme) decision to list singapore as a delivery point for the lme's new dollar-denominated aluminium contract. tay thiam peng, manager for international trading at the trade development board, said the decision would boost singapore's image as a major delivery port. "we hope this will encourage more metal traders to set up shop here and that singapore can become a delivery port for other metals as well," he said. the new contract, to start trading on june 1, is lme's first dollar-contract. reuter 
