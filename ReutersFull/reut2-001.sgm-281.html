norfolk southern corp said it entered into a joint venture with sovran financial corp to build the sovran iii building, which will be renamed norfolk southern tower. norfolk southern said it will locate its headquarters in the tower and occupy the 17th through 21st floors. it did not disclose the financial terms of the venture. the company said construction of the building adjacent to the royster building in downtown norfolk begain in october 1986. another story has been added to the original plans, making a 21-story tower. reuter 
