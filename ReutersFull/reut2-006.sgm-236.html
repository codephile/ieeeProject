calumet industries inc said it raised 4.2 mln dlrs from holders exercising company warrants. the program expired february 27. it issued 420,000 shares to holders who had exercised options to exchange four warrants and 10 dlrs for each share of common stock. about 87 pct of its warrants were exercised. these proceeds and another 14 mln dlrs from the sale of notes will provide long term financing for its hydrocal 2 heavy hydrotreating system under construction at its princeton, la., refinery. reuter 
