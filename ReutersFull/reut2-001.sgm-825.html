georgia gulf corp said it filed with the securities and exchange commission for a secondary offering of 4.8 mln shares of common stock, which will be sold by general electric co's general electric credit corp unit and georgia-pacific corp . the company said four mln of the shares will be sold in the united states and 800,000 shares will be offered abroad. goldman, sachs and co is the sole manager of the underwriting syndicate for the offering, it added. reuter 
