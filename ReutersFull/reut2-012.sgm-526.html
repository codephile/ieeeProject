thermo electron corp is raising 75 mln dlrs via an offering of convertible subordinated debentures due 2012 with a 5-3/4 pct coupon and par pricing, said lead manager shearson lehman brothers inc. the debentures are convertible into the company's stock at 30.50 dlrs per share, representing a premium of 25.77 pct over the stock price when terms on the debt were set. non-callable for two years, the issue is rated ba-3 by moody's investors and bb by standard and poor's. drexel burnham and tucker anthony co-managed the deal. reuter 
