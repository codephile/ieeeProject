york research corp said it has completed the arrangement of 11 mln dlrs of common stock to european investors. york said it will use the 11 mln dlrs as a construction pool to initially fund a series of cogeneration projects which it willk design, build, partially own and operate, with six projects already committed. gross revenues from the first six projects are expected to exceed 500 mln dlrs over their life cycle, york said, with revenue and income expected to start flowing in 1988. each project consists of a 20-year energy sale agreement, it said. reuter 
