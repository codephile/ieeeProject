mesa offshore trust said it received its first cash distribution from the mesa limited partnership and expects dividends to its unitholders to begin in july. it said the cash distribution for march totaled 2,484,000 dlrs, which will be used to repay a portion of the trust's outstanding 3.2 mln dlr line of credit with texas commerce bank national association. mesa said the line of credit must be repaid before distributions to unit holders may begin. mesa offshore trust previously owed mesa limited partnship for expenses related to exploration and development of the trust's properties. reuter 
