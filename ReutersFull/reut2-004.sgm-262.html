herley microwave systems inc said it won a series of contract additions and new awards worth 7.7 mln dlrs. it said the contracts were from the u.s. navy and airforce, martin marietta corp , and northrop corp . the orders are scheduled to be delivered in 1987 and early 1988. the company said its jan 31 backlog was 20 mln dlrs against 21 mln dlrs a year ago. reuter 
