gte corp's gte eft services inc unit and indiana national corp said they have signed an agreement where gte will provide electronic funds transfer switching services for indiana national's moneymover 24-hour teller and plus system networks. the companies said gte eft service will make an initial hookup with commerceamerica banking company, an affiliate of indiana national. the moneymover and plus system networks support about 400 24-hour automated teller machines in more than 80 cities in indiana and kentucky, the companies said. reuter 
