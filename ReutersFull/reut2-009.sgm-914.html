mobil corp said it will spend over 30 mln dlrs to upgrade a gasoline-producing unit at its beaumont, texas, refinery. it said the unit is a catalytic reformer, which converts low-octane components of gasoline into high-octane components for use in super unleaded gasoline. the company said the modernization will allow the unit to regenerate catalysts on a continuous basis without shutdown. currently, it must be shut twice a year. the unit produces 46,000 barrels of gasoline components a year. construction will start late this year, with completion set for mid-1989. reuter 
