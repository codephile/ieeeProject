dutch steel concern said it had reached agreement with nv philips gloeielampenfabrieken on its takeover of cirex, a specialised philips precision wax moulding unit. neither party would reveal financial details of the deal, initially announced in october. hoogovens said cirex turnover had grown in recent years to 30 mln guilders in 1986 and it expected further expansion. the unit delivers mainly to the car industry. hoogovens said the acquisition would strengthen its position as a supplier to industry of high-value metal products. reuter 
