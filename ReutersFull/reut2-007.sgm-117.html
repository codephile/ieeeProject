bank of tokyo curacao holdings nv is issuing a 120 mln canadian dlr eurobond due april 27, 1994 with an 8-1/2 pct coupon and priced at 101-1/8 pct, bank of tokyo international ltd said as lead manager. the bonds, guaranteed by bank of tokyo ltd, will be issued in denominations of 5,000 dlrs and will be listed in luxembourg. fees consist of 5/8 pct for management and underwriting combined and 1-1/4 pct for selling. co-lead is pru-bache securities. reuter 
