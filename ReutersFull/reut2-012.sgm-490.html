nippon signal co ltd's 50 mln swiss franc five-year notes issue with warrants has been priced at 1-1/4 pct compared with the originally indicated 1-3/8 pct, lead manager morgan stanley sa said. each 50,000-franc bond carries 81 warrants entitling the holder to buy 100 nippon signal shares for 594 yen each. the shares last traded at 579 yen. the swiss franc/yen exchange rate was fixed at 97.27. reuter 
