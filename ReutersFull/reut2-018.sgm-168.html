intelligent systems corp's asher technologies inc said it introduced two facsimile products, a portable unit and an internal card for ibm personal computers and compatibles. the jt facsimile products are designed for use with the ibm pc, xt, at, or compatibles to send or receive group iii transmissioins, the company said. asher said the new products eliminate printing out hard copies and manually sending each sheet to be transmitted. the internal unit retails for 395 dlrs, while the portable unit sells for 495 dlrs, the company said. reuter 
