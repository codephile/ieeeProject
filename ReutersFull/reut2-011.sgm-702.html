the u.s. treasury said it will auction 17 billion dlrs of 9-day and 20-day cash management bills on april 2. the auction will consist of 11 billion dlrs in 9-day bills and six billion dlrs in 20-day bills. bids for the bills will be received at all federal reserve banks and branches. tenders must be for a minimum of one mln dlrs, and non-competitive tenders from the public will not be accepted. bids will not be received at the treasury department. the 9-day bills will be issued april 7 and mature april 16, while the 20-day bills will be issued april 3 and mature april 23. reuter 
