unemployment in the u.k. fell a provisional seasonally-adjusted 44,100 in february, to total 3.07 mln or 11.1 pct of the workforce, the employment department said. in january, seasonally adjusted unemployment fell by a revised 1,100 to 11.3 pct, it said. initially the january position was reported as flat. the unadjusted jobless total, including school leavers, fell to 3.23 mln, or 11.7 pct, from 3.30 mln, 11.9 pct, in january. february was the seventh successive month that seasonally adjusted unemployment registered a fall. it was at a peak of 11.7 pct last summer. lord young, the employment minister, said there appeared little doubt the monthly fall, which has been running at about 20,000, will continue. a department spokesman said the sharp fall in february reflected some compensation for the flat figure in january and continued the downward trend seen in the past six months. he said the february fall was spread throughout the country and among men and women. reuter 
