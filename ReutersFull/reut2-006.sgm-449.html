the city of rotterdam today signed an agreement in principle to cooperate with the aomi cargo distribution centre in tokyo. acting mayor roel den dunnen said that cooperation between private and public entities in the tokyo and rotterdam area, and a fruitful exchange of information have a favourable influence on the flow of goods and services between the two countries. the aomi cargo distribution center, which will start operating in october this year, signed a similar agreement with rotterdam's twinned-port of seattle last september. reuter 
