the world bank said it has loaned uganda 13 mln dlrs through the international development association (ida), the bank's concessionary lending affiliate. the ida loan will support a project that hopes to preserve the country's natural forests and meet its demand for wood products by rehabilitating uganda's forestry management agency, the bank said. it also said the project plans to increase the area and management of protected forests, establish pilot wood farms and nurseries, and rehabilitate soft wood plantations. reuter 
