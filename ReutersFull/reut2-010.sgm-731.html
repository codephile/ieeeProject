quebecor inc , the canadian publishing concern, is issuing a 60 mln dlr convertible bond due may 14, 1997 bearing an indicated coupon of 5-3/4 to six pct, said merrill lynch capital markets as lead manager. the issue will be priced to have a conversion premium of 22 to 25 pct, with final terms to be set on monday, april 6. the company's stock last traded on the toronto stock exchange at 19-3/8. the securities will be listed in luxembourg and are available in denominations of 1,000 and 10,000 dlrs. fees include a one pct combined management and underwriting and a one pct selling concession. reuter 
