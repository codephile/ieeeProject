"despite the recent announcement of the suspension of interest payments by brazil, virtually all of the bank's loans to this country were income producing up to january 31, 1987, and there was little adverse impact on the bank's earnings prior to the announcement," commerce bank said. the bank did not disclose a forecast of the impact on future earnings by brazil's suspension of interest payments. reuter 
