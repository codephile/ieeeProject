carter-day industries inc said it reached an eight mln dlrs settlement in a suit filed in the bankruptcy court for the southern district of n.y. against five power companies. the suit was filed against montana power co , puget sound power and light co , portland general electric co , washington water power co and pacificorp's pacific power and light co. carter-day said proceeds from the settlement will be used to restore the company's liquidity, working capital and net worth. reuter 
