germantown savings bank said it has started a subscription offering of 3,500,000 shares at a maximum price of 11.50 dlrs each to customers and community residents. it said the offer will expire april 15. the company said any shares not sold will be offered to the public through underwriter alex. brown and sons inc , and the stock is expected to trade on the nasdaq system. it said the actual price to be charged for the shares will be determined after the subscription offering. reuter 
