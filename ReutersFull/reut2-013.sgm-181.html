a group of new york investors said it lowered its stake in rospatch corp to 170,250 shares, or 7.0 pct of the total outstanding common stock, from 202,108 shares, or 8.3 pct. in a filing with the securities and exchange commission, the group said it sold a net 31,858 shares of rospatch common stock between february 2 and march 31 at prices ranging from 21.50 to 25.13 dlrs a share. the group includes brookehill equities inc, a brokerage firm, and moore, grossman and derose inc, an investment advisor. reuter 
