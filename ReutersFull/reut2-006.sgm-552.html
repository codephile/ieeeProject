lyphomed inc said the food and drug administration gave it approval to market vancomycin hcl, an antibiotic used in hospitals to treat life-threatening infections. it said the product is currently marketed by eli lilly and co under the trade name vancocin. sales in 1986 were 110 mln dlrs. lyphomed said its objective is to get a 15 to 20 pct share of the market. reuter 
