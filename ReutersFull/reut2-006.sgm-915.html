allen-bradley co, a unit of rockwell international corp, said it has formed a joint venture with tdk corp of tokyo to produce, market and sell ferrite magnets. allen-bradley will be responsible for production and tdk will handle sales and marketing. products will be marketed through tdk corp of america, a chicago-based tdk unit that markets tdk's electronic components. the venture, to be known as allen-bradley/tdk magnetics, will be based in shawnee, okla, in the existing allen-bradley magnetics production facility. reuter 
