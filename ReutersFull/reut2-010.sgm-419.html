plenum publishing corp said it filed with the securities and exchange commission a registration covering the planned offering of 50 mln dlrs principal amount of convertible subordinated debentures due april 15, 2007. the company said it will grant the underwriter, bear stearns and co inc, a 30 day option to buy up to 7.5 mln dlrs principal amount of additional debentures to cover overallotment. it said proceeds will be used for acquisitions. reuter 
