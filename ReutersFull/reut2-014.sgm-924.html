thyssen ag unit thyssen guss ag, a metals group, said it had agreed to cooperate with canadian firm to manufacture fine casting products. both companies would merge plants in the west german towns of soest and moers under a new company in which the two groups would have equal shares in a capital of three mln marks. a spokeswoman for thyssen industrie ag, which holds 100 pct of thyssen guss, said the joint-venture should produce turnover of an annual 40 mln marks. thyssen industrie is held 90 pct by thyssen ag. reuter 
