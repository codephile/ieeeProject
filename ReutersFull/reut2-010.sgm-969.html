genrad inc said it introduced a software product that allows for development of test programs for its 227x line of in-circuit/functional test systems on digital equipment corp's vax-based computers. genrad said the product, named atg-32, provides for off-line development of test systems at four- to eight-times the speed of its current 227x test program. it said atg-32 is priced at 30,000 dlrs and is available immediately. reuter 
