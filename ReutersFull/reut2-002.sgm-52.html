equatorial communications co, a satellite data network concern, said it has signed an agreement with contel corp's , contel asc, unit allowing contel asc to buy 3.6 mln shares of equatorial stock at 3.25 dlrs per share. in addition, equatorial said under the agreement contel asc would buy a minimum of 10 mln dlrs of equipment from equatorial, loan equatorial up to six mln dlrs and assume a portion of equatorial's rights and obligations under a satellite transponder lease. reuter 
