prime computer inc said it introduced an interactive three dimensional graphics workstation. prime said it is its first offering in a new engineering/scientific product line and employs prime's implementation of att's unix system v.3 operating system, a program that controls the computer. prime said the new product delivers up to twice the performance of the iris 3120 series, considered the industry's leading 3-d graphics workstation, and is driven by five times the performances of a vax 11/780 system. shipment is scheduled for june, 1987, and the product's standard configuration is priced at 74,900 dls with a monthly maintenance fee of 492 dlrs, prime said. reuter 
