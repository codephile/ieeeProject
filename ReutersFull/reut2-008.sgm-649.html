asea capital corp bv is issuing a 10 billion yen bond due april 23, 1992 carrying a coupon of 4-1/2 pct and priced at 102-3/8, mitsui finance international ltd said as lead manager. the securities, which are guaranteed by parent company asea capital, will be listed on the luxembourg stock exchange. there is a put and a call option in 1990 at par. the securities are available in denominations of one mln yen each and are payable on april 23, 1987. the company's outstanding issues are rated aa-minus by standard and poor's corp. there is a 1-1/4 pct selling concession and a 5/8 pct combined management and underwriting fee. reuter 
