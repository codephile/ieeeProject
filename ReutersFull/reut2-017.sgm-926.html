british petroleum lp gas international said it cut its north sea lpg (liquified petroleum gas) posted price for propane to 95 dlrs from 110 dlrs a tonne, effective june 1. the move follows a similar cut to 92 from 108 dlrs a tonne announced by shell u.k. ltd friday, also fob north sea terminals and effective june 1 until further notice. both companies left their butane prices unchanged at 123 dlrs a tonne. a bp statement said its cut came "as a result of a price review occasioned by substantial changes in the lpg market." bp last lowered its posted propane price from 175 dlrs a tonne on march 1, and shell u.k.'s last change was from 113 dlrs may 1. reuter 
