coca-cola enterprises inc said it filed with the securities and exchange commission a registration statement covering 500 mln dlrs of debt. the company said it plans to sell 250 mln dlrs of notes due 1997 and an equal amount of debentures due 2017. proceeds will be used to refinance outstanding indebtedness, coca-cola enterprises said. the company named allen and co inc as lead manager and merrill lynch capital markets and salomon brothers inc as co-managers of the offerings. reuter 
