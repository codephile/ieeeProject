murgold resources inc said assays received from 320 feet of drifting on the number-three vein at the chester township property south of timmins, ontario, indicate an average of 0.528 ounce of gold per ton across an average width of five feet for the 320-foot length. the last working face assayed 0.422 ounce of gold per ton across 8.5 feet and the drift will be continued eastward from that point, the company said. reuter 
