west german wholesale turnover provisionally totalled about 58 billion marks in february, a rise of a real one pct compared with the same month last year, the federal statistics office said. turnover fell a nominal seven pct from february last year, down by around four billion marks, it added. turnover of about 114 billion marks in the first two months of 1987 represented a real decline of two pct from the same 1986 period. according to final figures, wholesale turnover had fallen by a real 5.7 pct in january, an office spokeswoman said. reuter 
