nationwide building society said it is issuing 30 mln stg of index-linked loan stock due 2021 by way of a placing on a yield basis in the domestic sterling market. the issue's real rate of return, price and the amount of the first interest payment will be fixed at 1400 gmt today. the real rate of return will be the sum of 0.60 pct plus the gross real rate of return on the 2-1/2 pct index-linked treasury stock due 2020. the issue will be partly paid with 25 stg pct payable on acceptance and the balance on july 1, 1987. reuter 
