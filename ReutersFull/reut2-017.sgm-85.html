chrysler corp said it and regie nationale des usines renault agreed to set may 5 as the new target date when chrysler aimed at reaching a definitive agreement for chrysler for acquire american motors corp. amc is 46.1 pct owned by renault. the two companies originally had targeted april 23 as completion date for reaching a definitive agreement. chrysler, which signed a letter of intent on march 9 to acquire amc, said "considerable progress has been made, but a number of issues remain to be resolved." reuter 
