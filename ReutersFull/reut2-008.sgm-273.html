westdeutsche landesbank girozentrale (westlb said it is raising the amount of a 14-3/8 pct five-year bullet australian dlr eurobond, priced at 101-1/2, to 75 mln dlrs from 50 mln. the bond, for westlb's westlb finance nv subsidiary, also will have an additional co-manager to the 20 already accepted into the deal, westlb said. westlb is lead manager, with hambros bank ltd as co-lead. other terms of the bond, launched on march 19, remain the same, the bank said. reuter 
