mark iv industries inc , which has said it may consider a bid to seek control of baird corp, said baird has not agreed to hold off on any defensive measures without giving mark iv at least 24 hours notice. in a filing with the securities and exchange commission, mark iv corrected a statement it made yesterday in another sec filing in which it listed several agreements reached with baird, including that baird would not take any anti-takeover steps without providing at least 24 hours notice. mark iv said it was later told by baird that baird had given no such assurance. reuter 
