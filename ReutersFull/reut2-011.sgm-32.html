society for savings said it will offer up to 100 mln dlrs of medium-term notes with maturites of one to five years through goldman, sachs and co and shearson lehman brothers inc. the notes will be supported by an irrevocable letter of credit by the federal home loan bank of boston. each note will bear interest at a fixed-rate or variable rate formula established at the time of issuance, society for savings said. bankers trust co will act as fiscal and paying agent. non-redeemable to maturity, the notes will be issued only in registered form and in minimum denomiations of 100,000 dlrs. reuter 
