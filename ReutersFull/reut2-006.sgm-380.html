dunhill holdings plc, a subsidiary of rothmans international plc has appointed chemical bank international ltd as sole dealer for a 50 mln u.s. dlr and sterling commercial paper program, chemical said. the paper will be sold in denominations of 500,000 dlrs and have maturities of seven to 364 days. chemical bank (london branch) will act as issuing and paying agent. the financing is the first of its kind for the company, which makes luxury consumer products. reuter 
