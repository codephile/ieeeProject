british printing and communication corporation plc has proposed the cancellation of all its preference shares in return for cash payments totalling 14.8 mln stg. bpcc said in a statement, "in view of the enlarged ordinary share capital and the improved financial position of the company, the board considers that the relatively small amount of preference share capital now represents capital in excess of the company's wants." shareholder approval will be sought at a meeeting on november 10.if granted, court confirmation will be required. reuter 
