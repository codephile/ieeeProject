bankers trust international ltd said it is issuing 200,000 stg call/dlr put currency warrants at an initial offer price of 31.25 dlrs per warrant. the issue is guaranteed by bankers trust new york corp. each warrant entitles the holder to exchange 500 dlrs for stg at a rate of 1.60 stg/dlr and the minimum number of warrants exercisable or tradeable is 100. exercise period for the warrants will run from april 22, 1987 until march 17, 1989. payment is due april 15, 1987. reuter 
