klm royal dutch airlines , in a move following similar steps recently by u.s. carrier twa, said it is cutting its fares on transatlantic flights from april 1. from april 1, a return fare amsterdam-new york will be cut by eight per cent to 1,099 guilders for the low season until mid-june, when seasonal increases will come into effect. klm recently rejected invitations by twa to coordinate price cuts on the amsterdam route and a spokesman said klm had only moved now to start up a nationwide promotional campaign in the netherlands to boost travel to the u.s. reuter 
