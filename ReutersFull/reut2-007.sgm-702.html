amax inc said it has filed for an offering of 15,757,350 common shares, including 757,300 to be sold by mitsui and co ltd . amax said it will offer 12,607,350 shares in the u.s. through underwriters led by first boston inc , along with american express co's shearson lehman brothers inc subsidiary, and merrill lynch and co inc . remaining shares will be sold outside the u.s. through underwriters led by first boston's credit suisse first boston ltd affiliate, shearson, goldman sachs and merrill lynch. amax said it will use its proceeds to reduce revolving credit debt and repurchase or repay from time to time other debt. reuter 
