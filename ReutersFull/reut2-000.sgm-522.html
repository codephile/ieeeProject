sony (u.k.) ltd said it would be doubling capacity at its bridgend, wales, television and components factory over the next three years in a 30 mln stg expansion. the expansion, backed by welsh office grants, would make bridgend sony's biggest tv manufacturing plant in europe with output of about 500,000 tv sets per year, a spokesman said. the move will add 300 jobs at bridgend, bringing the total workforce to 1,500 at the end of the three-year period. the expansion is part of a wider move by sony corp to locate more production capacity nearer its markets, a spokesman said. reuter 
