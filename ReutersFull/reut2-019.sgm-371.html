liposome co inc at its annual meeting announced the formation of a canadian subsidiary, canadian liposome co ltd. it said the company cements a relationship with university of british columbia researcher pieter cullis and his scientific group who are developing anti-cancer products for liposome. the company also said lead bankers completed liposome's stock offering of one mln shares on june 11 at 6.875 dlrs a share. liposome said it netted 7,388,750 dlrs. reuter 
