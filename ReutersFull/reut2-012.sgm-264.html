general motors corp said it produced 395,294 cars in the u.s. in march, up from 366,671 in march 1986. the automaker said it made 144,959 trucks last month, an increase from 131,640 produced in the same month last year. year-to-date u.s. car output through the end of march was 1,064,141 vehicles, down from last year's 1,205,768. year-to-date truck production was 395,967, down from 410,821. reuter 
