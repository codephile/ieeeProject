capital cities/abc's abc television network said its newsone news service reached a cooperative agreement with potomac telecommunications group to offer abc affiliates expanded coverage of washington news. potomac will work with abc to transmit stories at rates lower than current transmitting costs. potomac covers washington for 150 stations. the deal affects about 219 abc affiliates, and does not affect potomac's other customers. the deal does not affect abc's network news coverage, which is currently involved in a strike. reuter 
