mcdowell enterprises inc said its auditors have decided to qualify their opinion on 1986 financial statements due to questions about the company's ability to continue as a going concern. mcdowell said it will file its annual report for 1986 by april 15, after applying for an extension, and will report a loss for the year of about six mln dlrs after a substantial writedown of assets and an increase in costs associated with its withdrawal from the construction business. in 1985, mcdowell lost 2,152,000 dlrs before a 1,240,000 dlr loss from discontinued operations. reuter 
