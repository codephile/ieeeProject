ownes-illinois holdings corp filed with the securities and exchange commission for debt offerings with principal amounts totalling 2.05 billion dlrs. the company, which is the successor to ownens-illinois inc, said it plans to offer 250 mln dlrs of senior subordinated notes due 1996, 500 mln dlrs of subordinated debentures due 1999 and 1.3 billion dlrs of junior subordinated discount debentures due 2003. proceeds from the offering, together with about 50 mln dlrs in bank borrowings, will be used to repay or redeem financing incurred in connection with the takeover, it said. owens-illinois, which was taken over by a group led by kohlberg kravis roberts and co, said financing incurred in the takeover includes a 550 mln dlr bridge loan provided by a bank group, 200 mln dlrs provided by two common stock partnerships and 600 mln dlrs of junior subordinated notes due sept 17, 1988 which were sold to an affiliated of morgan stanley and co inc. other proceeds will be used to pay underwriting costs, the company said. morgan stanley is the underwriter, it said. reuter 
