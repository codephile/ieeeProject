a euro-commercial paper program for provinsbanken a/s of denmark has been increased to 250 mln dlrs from the original 100 mln, merrill lynch capital markets said. the program allows the danish bank to issue euro-certificates of deposit with maturities of up to 365 days. merrill lynch will continuue to act as dealer for the program, with shearson lehman brothers international and s.g. warburg and co ltd as co-dealers. reuter 
