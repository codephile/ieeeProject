neti technologies inc said american telephone and telegraph co agreed to sell neti's document transfer software. neti chairman lawrence brilliant said in response to an inquiry that the software, called "docuforum", transfers documents between separate locations over telephone lines. it is designed to work with most personal computers, including those made by international business machines co . brilliant said there will be a one-time set-up charge of 1,000 dlrs for the software, which will cost 40 dlrs per hour to use. there will be a 250 dlr monthly minimum use charge. reuter 
