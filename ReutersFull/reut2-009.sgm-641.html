campbell resources inc said it raised its voting stake in to about 64 pct from 52 pct through acquisition of another 870,000 meston lake shares in its previously reported takeover bid. campbell, in its bid that expired march 23, offered 80 cts cash and 1.25 "legended" campbell shares for each meston share. the legended shares are not tradeable for one year. it said the 3.4 mln meston shares not tendered in the offer were held by about 550 stockholders, including quebec's la societe de developpement de la baie james, with 1.3 mln shares. reuter 
