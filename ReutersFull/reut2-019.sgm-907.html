warrantech corp said it agreed with providence washington insurance group to insure warrantech's obligations on its extended service line of business. the company said that last year it wrote over 1.9 mln dlrs of extended service plans representing over 20 mln dlrs' worth of electronic products. the company said the agreement will enhance its future business opportunities, as an important aspect for it is the flexibility, credibility and aggressiveness of its insurance company. reuter 
