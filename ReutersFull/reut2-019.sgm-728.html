the u.s. environmental protection agency said it denied a request by wheeling-pittsburgh steel corp that it be allowed to continue to operate the follansbee, w. va., sinter plant while court-ordered pollution control equipment is installed. the epa said that since the original consent decree with the company was signed in 1979, the company had had numerous opportunities to reduce harmful emmissions from the plant. the agency said that only after the company failed to comply by the last available extension of its compliance deadline did the agency ask the court to close the plant. reuter 
