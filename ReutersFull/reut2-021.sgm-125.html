global motors inc said it has agreed to become become the exclusive north american importer of the lincan gama, a four-wheel-drive sport vehicle manufactured by of indonesia. in a letter of intent it signed with p.t. indauda, global said it expects to begin marketing the first lincan gamas in mid-1989, with 20,000 units projected to be marketed in the first year. global is the parent company of yugo america inc, importer of the yugoslavian yugo vehicle. reuter 
