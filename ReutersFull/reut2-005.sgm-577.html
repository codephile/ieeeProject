broken hill proprietary co ltd said it will integrate utah international inc, which it bought three years ago, under a common management structure. the company said effective june 1, when utah international chairman alexander wilson retires, james curry will become chairman and chief executive officer and a new bhp-utah minerals international board will be named. curry is currently executive vice president of utah international. bhp petroleum (americas) inc, formerly part of utah international, will become a subsidiary of bhp's renamed bhp petroleum international, headquartered in melbourne. reuter 
