gte corp filed with the securities and exchange commission for a shelf offering of four mln shares of auction preferred stock on terms to be set at the time of sale. the dividend rate on the shares is reset by auction every 49 days. the shares will be offered in units of 1,000 and each unit will have a liquidation preference of 100,000 dlrs. an underwriting group will be led by goldman, sachs and co. gte said proceeds will be used to reduce short-term debt and to partially fund repurchases of common stock. reuter 
