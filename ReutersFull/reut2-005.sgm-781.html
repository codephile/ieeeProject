thailand's m-1 money supply rose 9.8 pct to an estimated 117.3 billion baht in january after increasing 6.6 pct in december, the bank of thailand said. it said year-on-year m-1 grew 23.1 pct in january after an 18.5 pct increase in december. the central bank said m-2 rose 1.7 pct to an estimated 686.5 billion baht in january after a 2.4 pct increase in december while year-on-year it expanded 13.3 pct after a 12.6 pct rise in december. reuter 
