magnetic technologies corp said it expects the second half to show continued growth in earnings and sales. the company today reported a profit for the first half ended january 31 of 105,013 dlrs, compared with a year-earlier loss of 745,641 dlrs, on sales of 3,661,565 dlrs, up from 2,810,132 dlrs. in all of last year, magnetic earned 996,000 dlrs after a loss from discontinued operations of 359,000 dlrs, on sales of 6,084,000 dlrs. reuter 
