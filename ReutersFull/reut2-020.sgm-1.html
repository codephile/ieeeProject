nec corp will supply (mhs) of france with manufacturing technology for 16-bit microchips used in microcomputers, an nec spokesman said. mhs, a joint venture between france's matra and harris corp of the u.s., will manufacture and market globally a microcomputer based on nec's micron pd 78312 and micron pd78310 chips. mhs will pay nec an undisclosed sum for the technology. reuter 
