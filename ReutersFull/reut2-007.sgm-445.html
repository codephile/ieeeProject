public service co of indiana inc said it acted today to replace 35 mln dlrs in existing debt, estimating that would save it about 1.8 mln dlrs a year. the utility said it plans to issue a 35 mln dlr, 7.6 pct first mortgage bond, series nn, due 2012 to the city of princeton, ind. the series nn bonds will serve as collateral to princeton's equal-sized pollution control bond issue that are expected to have the same interest rate. proceeds from this issue will be used to redeem the utility's 12-3/4 pct bonds that serve as collateral for princeton's same-rate pollution control bonds. reuter 
