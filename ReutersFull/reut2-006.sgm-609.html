wyle laboratories said it is issuing in the euro-market 25 mln dlrs of 6-1/4 pct convertible subordinated debentures due march 2002. the debentures are convertible into wyle common stock at a rate of 19.25 dlrs worth of debentures for each common share, the company said. it said proceeds from the offering will be used for general corporate purposes, and to repay borrowings under the company's bank credit line. kidder peabody international ltd and montgomery securities are co-managing the offering, wyle also said. reuter 
