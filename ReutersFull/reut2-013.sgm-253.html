economy minister juan sourrouille was heading for washington for talks with creditor banks on rescheduling part of argentina's foreign debt. economy ministry sources said he hoped to seal an agreement rescheduling 30 bln dlrs of private foreign debt. argentina, seeking 2.15 bln dlrs in fresh loans from private international banks to meet 1987 growth targets, has been negotiating with the banks' steering committee since february. sourroille will also attend international monetary fund and world bank meetings. reuter 
