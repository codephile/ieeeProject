the federal home loan bank board said that it closed equitable savings and loan association of fountain valley, california, on friday due to insolvency, amongst other complaints. the bank board said in a statement that equitable's insured deposits have been transfered to a unit of buffalo-based empire of america federal savings bank in woodland hills, california. consequently, equitable's offices will open as empire branches today. equitable had assets of 59.12 mln dlrs. reuter 
