america pop inc said it plans to build with its joint venture partner, puissant group, a 2.6 mln dlrs 120-unit days inn motor lodge at the ohio state fairgrounds in columbus. construction is expected to be completed in late october. the company also said it plans to complete by mid-october, construction of a 60-unit days inn in athens, ohio, and by november a 60-unit days inn motor lodge in green castle, pennsylvania. these two projects are organized as limited partnerships, it said. reuter 
