japanese primary aluminium imports rose to 98,170 tonnes in february from 91,157 in january and 94,926 a year earlier, the japan aluminium federation said. this brought total imports in 1986/87, ending march 31, to 1.09 mln tonnes against 1.25 mln a year earlier. the february total included 19,102 tonnes from the u.s. against 16,577 in january and 9,933 a year ago, 24,391 from australia against 19,585 and 21,208, and 12,611 from indonesia against 5,891 and 16,601. reuter 
