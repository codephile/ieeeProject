the world bank is issuing a 100 mln australian dlr bond due july 20, 1992 paying 13-3/8 pct and priced at 101-7/8 pct, lead manager hambros bank ltd said. the non-callable bond is available in denominations of 1,000 and 10,000 australian dlrs and will be listed in luxembourg. the selling concession is 1-1/4 pct while management and underwriting combined pays 5/8 pct. the payment date is july 20. reuter 
