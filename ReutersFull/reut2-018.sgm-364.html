matsushita electric industrial co ltd , mazda motor corp and ford motor co have signed an agreement to set up a joint venture to make car air conditioners in japan, matsushita said in a statement. the new venture, will be capitalised at 1.5 billion yen, and the three partners will hold equal shares. the partners will invest 3.6 billion yen in constructing a plant in hiroshima, due for completion in late 1988. the plant will initially make 100,000 air conditioners a year for mazda, it said. reuter 
