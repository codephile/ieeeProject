ltv corp's ltv steel corp said it agreed to resume negotiations with the united steelworkers of america at the local plant levels, to discuss those provisions of its proposal that require local implementation. the local steelworker union narrowly rejected a tentative agreement with the company on may 14, it said. ltv also said it agreed to reopen its offer contained in the tentative agreement reached with the union's negotiating committee as part of a plan to resolve problems through local discussions. reuter 
