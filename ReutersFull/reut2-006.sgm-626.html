the world bank is issuing 150 mln australian dlrs of zero coupon bonds due april 15, 1992 priced at 53, lead manager hambros bank ltd said. the bonds will be listed in luxembourg and sold in denominations of 1,000 and 10,000 dlrs. payment date is april 15. the co-lead managers are orion royal bank ltd and citicorp investment bank ltd. fees are 3/4 pct for selling and 1/2 pct for management and underwriting. there also is a 1/8 pct praecipuum. reuter 
