consumers power co is raising 250 mln dlrs through a two-tranche offering of first mortgage bonds, said lead manager morgan stanley and co inc. a 150 mln dlr offering due 1992 was given a nine pct coupon and par pricing to yield 112.5 basis points over comparable treasuries. this tranche is non-callable for life. a companion 100 mln dlr issue maturing in 1997 was assigned a 9-5/8 pct coupon and priced at 98.50 to yield 9.703 pct, or 143 basis points over treasuries. these bonds are non-callable for five years. the debt is rated baa-3 by moody's, bbb-minus by s and p. reuter 
