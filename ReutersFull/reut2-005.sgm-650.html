lyphomed inc said it and vestar inc entered into a joint venture to develop and market liposome formulations of selected drugs. liposomes are spheres of subcellular size which are composed of phospholipids, the primary components of living cell membranes. during the project's first year, the companies will jointly develop liposome formulations for designated drugs and establish a commercial production capacity as well as coordinate and fund clinical studies of the formulations. reuter 
