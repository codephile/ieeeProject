bangladesh's broad-based m2 money supply rose 6.95 pct to 132.79 billion taka in december after rising 0.51 pct to 124.16 billion in november and 3.07 pct to 114.22 billion in december 1985, the central bank said. on a year on year basis, m2 rose 16.25 pct in the 12 months to december, 12.04 pct to november and 16 pct to december 1985. narrowly-based m1 rose 9.55 pct to 50 billion taka against a fall of 0.45 pct to 45.64 billion in november and a 3.03 pct rise to 45.51 billion to december 1985, it added. year on year, m1 rose 9.86 pct in december. reuter 
