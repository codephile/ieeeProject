south korea signed with japan for a 44.6 billion yen credit facility, part of a four billion dlr aid package for south korea's economic development plan to 1988, foreign ministry officials here said. the facility, to be used mostly for building a multi-purpose dam and modernising educational and medical facilities and for sewer projects, bears interest of 4.25 pct per year and is repayable over 18 years after a grace period of seven years. reuter 
