iran's budget for the fiscal year starting on saturday forecasts revenue of 50.3 billion dlrs, a 15 pct increase from this year, figures published by the iranian news agency irna show. irna, quoting figures supplied by the head of the majlis (parliament) budget and planning committee, said spending for the war with iraq was up by 3.6 billion dlrs, but gave no overall figure. oil revenue has been set at 11.74 billion dlrs, with average exports of 1.5 mln barrels per day (bpd) at 16 dlrs per barrel. reuter 
