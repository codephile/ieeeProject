victory markets inc is raising 60 mln dlrs through an offering of subordinated debentures due 1999 with a 12-1/2 pct coupon and par pricing, said sole underwriter drexel burnham lambert inc. non-callable for five years, the debt is rated b-2 by moody's investors service inc. the issue was increased from an initial offering of 50 mln dlrs because of investor demand. reuter 
