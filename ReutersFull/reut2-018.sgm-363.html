daiwa singapore ltd, wholly-owned by daiwa securities co ltd of japan, yesterday launched a 100 mln u.s. dlr tranche of an equity warrant eurobond in singapore for japan's minebea co ltd . the five-year eurobond carries an indicative coupon rate of one and 5/8 pct and will be managed from singapore. the warrants may be exercised for shares in minebea from july 1, 1987 to june 10, 1992. the bond was part of a 200 mln u.s. dlr equity warrant eurobond issued by minebea. nomura international ltd launched the other 100 mln dlr bond in london. reuter 
