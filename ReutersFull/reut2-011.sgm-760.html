a brazilian delegation, led by central bank president francisco gros, will meet brazil's bank advisory committee here on april 10, said citibank's william rhodes as chairman of the 14-bank committee. rhodes said in a statement that the parties are expected to discuss brazil's economic plans and "interim arrangements." he did not elaborate further. this meeting will be the first here since brazil's unilateral suspension of interest payments on 68 billion dlrs of commercial bank debt on february 20. the two sides held inconclusive talks in miami on march 22, bankers said. reuter 
