adia services inc said it agreed to sell 500,000 shares of common stock to its principal stockholder, adia s.a., at a price based on the current market price. the sale will raise adia s.a.'s ownership in the company to 74.7 pct from 72.7 pct, adia said. the company also said it intends to use proceeds from the stock sale to retire debt, for working capital and for general corporate purposes. reuter 
