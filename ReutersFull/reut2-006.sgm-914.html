president reagan has sent congress proposals to streamline high-tech export control procedures, the white house said. "these proposals are part of the president's energetic program to enhance america's competitiveness in the world economy," it said in a written statement. it said the proposals included exempting from license requirements a number of low technology items and allowing foreign manufacturers to re-export u.s. parts and components up to a certain level without u.s. government re-export authorization. reuter 
