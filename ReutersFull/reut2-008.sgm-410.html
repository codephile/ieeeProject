lsi logic corp said it intends to offer 100 mln dlrs of convertible subordinated debentures due 2002, outside the u.s., through morgan stanley international and prudential-bache capital funding. the company said it is issuing the debentures with an indicated coupon range of 5-3/4 pct to 6-1/4 pct and an indicated conversion premium range of 24-26 pct. the debentures will be convertible into common stock through the life of the bonds. the company said the debt is expected to be listed on the luxembourg stock exchange. reuter 
