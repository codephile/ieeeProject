duff and phelps said it lowered the ratings on duquesne light company fixed income securities, affecting approximately 1.8 billion dlrs in debt. duquesne first mortgage bonds and debentures were lowered to dp-9 (middle bbb) from dp-8 (high bbb), preferred stock rating to dp-10 (low bbb) from dp-9 and preference stock to dp-11 (high bb) from dp-10. the change resulted from a denial by the pennsylvania public utility commission for a rate increase and an order by the commission to reduce rates by 18.6 mln dlrs annually. reuter 
