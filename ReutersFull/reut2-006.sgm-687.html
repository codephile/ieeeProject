valero energy corp said valero natural gas partners l.p. was making an initial public offering of 9.5 mln units at 22.75 dlrs a unit. it said a subsidiary will own a 49 pct stake in valero natural gas. the offering is being managed by shearson lehman bros inc, goldman sachs and co, e.f. hutton and co inc, and dean witter reynolds inc. valero natural gas was formed to buy valero energy's natural gas pipeline and liquid assets. it will use the proceeds from the offering and a 550 mln dlr private placement of first mortgage notes for the purchase. reuter 
