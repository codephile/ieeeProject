the new york coffee, sugar and cocoa exchange (csce) elected former first vice chairman gerald clancy to a two-year term as chairman of the board of managers, replacing previous chairman howard katz. katz, chairman since 1985, will remain a board member. clancy currently serves on the exchange board of managers as chairman of its appeals, executive, pension and political action committees. the csce also elected charles nastro, executive vice president of shearson lehman bros, as first vice chairman. anthony maccia, vice president of woodhouse, drake and carey, was named second vice chairman, and clifford evans, president of demico futures, was elected treasurer. reuter 
