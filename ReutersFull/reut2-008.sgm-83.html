indonesia will need a new generation of satellites by 1995 at the latest to link the giant archipelago of 13,000 islands, telecommunications minister achmad tahir was quoted as saying. speaking after the successful launch on friday of a new indonesian communications satellite from cape canaveral, florida, tahir said that a new generation of satellites was very important for indonesia's domestic communications system. he was quoted by indonesia's official antara news agency as saying the government would start work straight away on preparations and studies for the next generation of satellites. reuter 
